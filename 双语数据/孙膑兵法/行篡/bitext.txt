古文：孙子曰：用兵移民之道，权衡也。
现代文：孙膑说：动用民力去作战的问题，必须仔细斟酌。
English: Sun Yat-sen said: The question of using civilian forces to fight must be carefully considered.

古文：权衡，所以篡贤取良也。
现代文：斟酌的目的是为了真正选拔出有德有才的人。
English: The purpose of the consideration is to truly select the most virtuous and talented people.

古文：阴阳，所以聚众合敌也。
现代文：运用阴阳变化配合的规律，是为了聚集民众的力量去对敌。
English: The use of the law of yin and yang is to gather the strength of the people to fight the enemy.

古文：正衡再累既忠，是谓不穷。
现代文：不断校正安排资源调配使能维持运作，就能在供应上做到无尽。
English: Constantly calibrating the allocation of resources to sustain operations can be endless in supply.

古文：称乡县衡，虽其宜也。
现代文：私人和公有的财物要统一安排使用。
English: The use of private and public property should be uniformly arranged.

古文：私公之财壹也，夫民有不足于寿而有余于货者，有不足于货而有余于寿者。
现代文：民众之中，有的人财物很多却贪生怕死，有的财物少却不怕死。只有明智的君王和贤明的人，才能正确处理，适当动用民力，使得牺牲生命的人不怨恨，被征用财物的人也不生气。
English: Among the people, some people have a lot of wealth but are greedy for life and afraid of death, and some people have little wealth but are not afraid of death. Only a wise king and a wise person can handle it correctly and make appropriate use of people's power, so that those who sacrifice their lives do not resent and those who are expropriated are not angry.

古文：唯明王圣人知之，故能留之。
现代文：百姓都会尽自己的力量。如果亲近的人不肯尽力，那就不可能让别人尽力了。
English: People will do their best. If those close to them are not willing to do their best, then it is impossible for others to do their best.

古文：死者不毒，夺者不愠。
现代文：如果征用财物过多，就会伤害民众，从而导致民众对君王不满。
English: If the requisition of property is excessive, it will hurt the people, which will lead to the people's dissatisfaction with the king.

古文：此无穷民皆尽力。
现代文：征用财物少君主就会得到全国的拥护。
English: Without requisitioning property, the monarchy would receive nationwide support.

古文：近者弗则远者无能。
现代文：应该让百姓积累财物。
English: People should be allowed to accumulate wealth.

古文：货多则辨，辨则民不德其上。
现代文：我主张让百姓积累财物，是因为只有这样才能保证长时间用兵作战
English: I advocate letting the people accumulate property, because only in this way can we ensure long-term use of soldiers in combat.
