古文：将者不可以不义，不义则不严，不严则不威，不威则卒弗死。
现代文：军队的将领绝不能不公正，如不公正就不可能严格治军，冶军不严就没有威信，将领没有威信，那士兵就不会拼死效命。
English: The generals of an army must never be unjust. If they are unjust, it is impossible to govern the army strictly. If the army is not strict, there will be no authority. If the generals have no authority, then the soldiers will not fight to the death.

古文：故义者，兵之首也。
现代文：所以说，公正是统兵的首要条件，就像人必须有头一样。
English: Justice, therefore, is the first requisite of a general, just as a man must have a head.
