古文：子曰： 愚而好自用，贱而好自专。
现代文：孔子说： 愚昧却喜欢自以为是，卑贱但又好独断专行。
English: Confucius said: "Stupidity likes to be self-righteous, and meanness likes to be arbitrary."

古文：生乎今之世，反古之道。
现代文：生活在当下的时代，却一心想要恢复古代的做法。
English: Living in the modern age, but intent on reviving ancient practices.

古文：如此者灾及其身者也。 
现代文：这样做，灾祸一定会降临到自己的身上。 
English: If you do so, disaster will surely befall you.

古文：非天子不议礼，不制度，不考文。
现代文：不是天子，就不要议订礼仪，不要制订法度，不要考订文字规范。
English: If you are not the son of heaven, do not discuss etiquette, do not make laws, and do not set standards for writing.

古文：今天下，车同轨，书同文，行同伦。
现代文：现在普天下车辙统一，文字统一，伦理观念统一。
English: Now, the whole world has a unified railway, a unified written language, and a unified ethical concept.

古文：虽有其位，苟无其德，不敢作礼乐焉。虽有其德，苟无其位，亦不敢作礼乐焉。
现代文：虽有天子的地位，如果没有天子的德行，是不敢制作礼乐制度的；虽然有天子的德行，如果没有天子的地位，也是不敢制作礼乐制度的。
English: Although he has the status of the son of heaven, if he does not have the virtue of the son of heaven, he will not dare to make a ritual music system; although he has the virtue of the son of heaven, if he does not have the status of the son of heaven, he will not dare to make a ritual music system.

古文：子曰： 吾说夏礼，杞不足徵也。吾学殷礼，有宋存焉。吾学周礼，今用之。吾从周。 
现代文：孔子说： 我谈论夏朝的礼制，夏的后裔杞国已不足以验证它；我学习殷朝的礼制，仅仅有宋国保持着它；我学习周朝的礼制，现在还实行着它，所以我遵从周礼。 
English: Confucius said: I talk about the etiquette of the Xia Dynasty, but it is not enough for the descendants of the Xia Dynasty to validate it; I study the etiquette of the Yin Dynasty, only the Song Dynasty maintains it; I study the etiquette of the Zhou Dynasty, and I still practice it, so I follow the Zhou etiquette.
