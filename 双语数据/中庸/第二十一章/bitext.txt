古文： 自诚明，谓之性；自明诚，谓之教。
现代文：由内心真诚而自然明白道理，就叫做天性；由明白存在的意义而做到真诚，这就叫教化。
English: To be sincere from the heart and to understand the truth naturally is called nature; to be sincere from understanding the meaning of existence is called enlightenment.

古文：诚则明矣；明则诚矣。 
现代文：真诚就会通晓事理，通晓事理就会真诚。
English: Sincerity will lead to knowledge of the facts, and knowledge of the facts will lead to sincerity.
