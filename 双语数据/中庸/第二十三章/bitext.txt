古文：其次致曲。曲能有诚。
现代文：比圣人差一些的贤人，在细微处下工夫，也能达到真诚的境界。
English: The wise man, who is somewhat inferior to the sage, can also reach the realm of sincerity with a little effort.

古文：诚则形。形则著。著则明。明则动。动则变。变则化。
现代文：达到真诚就会表现出来，表现出来就会逐渐显著，显著了就会发扬光大，发扬光大就会感动他人，感动他人就会引起转变，引起转变就能化育万物。
English: If sincerity is achieved, it will be manifested, and if it is manifested, it will gradually become significant, and if it is significant, it will be carried forward, and if it is carried forward, it will move others, and if it moves others, it will cause transformation, and if it causes transformation, it can transform all things.

古文：唯天下至诚为能化。
现代文：只有天下最真诚的人能化育万物。
English: Only the most sincere person in the world can inspire people.
