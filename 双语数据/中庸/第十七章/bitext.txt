古文：子曰： 舜其大孝也与！
现代文：孔子说： 舜大概是最孝敬的人了。
English: From the subtle to the obvious, the sincerity of the heart cannot be hidden in this way.Confucius said: You are a most honorable person!

古文：德为圣人，尊为天子，富有四海之内。宗庙飨之，子孙保之。 
现代文：德行上是圣人，地位上是尊贵的天子，财富上拥有整个天下，宗庙里祭祀他，子子孙孙都继承他的功业。 
English: In virtue, he is a saint, in status, he is a noble heavenly son, in wealth, he owns the whole world, he is worshipped in his ancestral temple, and his children and grandchildren all inherit his achievements.

古文：故大德，必得其位，必得其禄，必得其名，必得其寿。
现代文： 所以，有高尚德行的人必会得到相应的地位，必定会得到相应的财富，必定得到他应有的名望，必然长寿。 
English: Therefore, a person with high moral conduct will surely get the corresponding status, will surely get the corresponding wealth, will surely get the reputation he deserves, and will surely live a long life.

古文：故天之生物必因其材而笃焉。
现代文： 所以，上天生养万物，必定根据它们的资质而厚待它们。
English: Therefore, since God created and nurtured all things, He must treat them well according to their qualities.

古文：故栽者培之，倾者覆之。
现代文：能成材的得到培育，不能成材的就遭到淘汰。 
English: So if you can grow them, grow them, and if they're crooked, make them crooked.

古文：诗曰： 嘉乐君子，宪宪令德，宜民宜人。受禄于天。
现代文： 《诗经》说： 高尚优雅的君子，有光明美好的德行，让人民安居乐业，享受上天赐予的福禄。
English: The "Song of Songs" says: Noble and elegant gentlemen, with bright and beautiful virtues, let the people live and work in peace and enjoy the blessings given by God.

古文：保佑命之，自天申之。 
现代文：上天保佑他、任用他，这是上天的意志啊。  
English: Heaven bless him, appoint him, this is the will of heaven.

古文：故大德者必受命。 
现代文： 所以，有高尚德行的人必定会承受天命。 
English: Therefore, a man of great virtue must be predestined.
