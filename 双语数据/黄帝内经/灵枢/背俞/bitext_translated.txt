古文：黄帝问于岐伯曰：愿闻五脏之腧，出于背者。
现代文：黄帝问岐伯道：我想知道五脏的俞穴都处于背的什么部位。
English: Huang Di asked Qi Bo: I want to know what part of the back is the anus of the five internal organs.

古文：岐伯曰：背中大腧，在杼骨之端，肺腧在三焦之间，心腧在五焦之间，膈腧在七焦之间，肝腧在九焦之间，脾腧在十一焦之间，肾腧在十四焦之间。
现代文：岐伯说：背中大俞在项后第一椎骨下的两旁，肺俞在第三椎骨的两旁，心俞在第五椎骨的两旁，膈俞在第七椎骨的两旁，肝俞在第九椎骨的两旁，脾俞在第十一椎骨的两旁，肾俞在第十四椎骨的两旁，这些俞穴都是挟持脊柱两旁彼此相距三寸。
English: Qi Bo said: the dorsal fins are on both sides under the first vertebra, the pulmonary fins are on both sides of the third vertebra, the heart fins are on both sides of the fifth vertebra, the gills are on both sides of the seventh vertebra, the liver fins are on both sides of the ninth vertebra, the spleen fins are on both sides of the eleventh vertebra, and the renal fins are on both sides of the fourteenth vertebra. These fins are three inches apart from each other on both sides of the spine.

古文：皆挟脊相去三寸所，则欲得而验之，按其处，应在中而痛解，乃其输也。
现代文：如果想检验穴之所在，就用手按压其部位，病人感到里面酸痛，或是原有酸痛，通过按压此处而缓解，就是背俞穴所在。
English: If you want to check the location of the hole, press the area with your hand. The patient feels soreness inside, or the original soreness is relieved by pressing here, which is where the dorsal fin hole is located.

古文：灸之则可，刺之则不可。
现代文：用背俞穴治病，可以使用灸法，不可贸然使用针刺之法。
English: To cure the disease with the dorsal fistula, the moxibustion method can be used, and the method of acupuncture should not be used recklessly.

古文：气盛则泻之，虚则补之。
现代文：在取用背俞穴进行灸疗时，邪气盛的就用泻法，正气虚的就用补法。
English: When using the dorsal fistula for moxibustion, use the laxation method for evil spirits and the supplementation method for virtuous spirits.

古文：以火补者，毋吹其火，须自灭也；以火泻之，疾吹其火，传其艾，须其火灭也。
现代文：用艾火行补法时，不要吹艾火，要让艾火自行燃烧和熄灭；用艾火行泻法时，要急吹艾火使燃烧旺盛，然后拍艾条，待其火苗灭后再灸。
English: When performing the mending method with mugwort, do not blow the mugwort, let the mugwort burn and extinguish itself; when performing the laxative method with mugwort, blow the mugwort quickly to make it burn vigorously, then beat the mugwort, wait for its flame to die down, and then moxibustion.
