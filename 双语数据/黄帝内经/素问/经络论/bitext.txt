古文：黄帝问曰：夫络脉之见也，其五色各异，青黄赤白黑不同，其故何也？
现代文：黄帝问道：络脉显露在外面，五色各不相同，有青、黄、赤、白、黑的不同，这是什么缘故呢？
English: The Yellow Emperor asked, "What is the reason why the veins are exposed on the outside, and the five colors are different, and there are differences between blue, yellow, red, white, and black?

古文：岐伯对曰：经有常色，而络无常变也。
现代文：岐伯回答说：经脉的颜色经常不变，而络脉则没有常色，常随四时之气变而变。
English: Qi Bo replied, "The color of the meridians often does not change, while that of the vas deferens does not have a constant color, but often changes with the four o'clock wind.

古文：帝曰：经之常色何如？
现代文：黄帝说：经脉的常色是怎样的呢？
English: Huang Di said: What is the normal color of the meridians?

古文：岐伯曰：心赤，肺白，肝青，脾黄，肾黑，皆亦应其经脉之色也。
现代文：岐伯说：心主赤，肺主白，肝主青，脾主黄，肾主黑，这些都是与其所属经脉的常色相应的。
English: Qi Bo said: The heart is mainly red, the lungs are mainly white, the liver is mainly blue, the spleen is mainly yellow, and the kidney is mainly black. These all correspond to the normal color of the meridians they belong to.

古文：帝曰：络之阴阳，亦应其经乎？
现代文：黄帝说：阴络与阳络，也与其经脉的主色相应吗？
English: The Yellow Emperor said, "Do yin and yang also correspond to the main color of their meridians?

古文：岐伯曰：阴络之色应其经，阳络之色变无常，随四时而行也。
现代文：岐伯说：阴络的颜色与其经脉相应，阳络的颜色则变化无常，它是随着四时的变化而变化的，这都是正常的，是无病的表现。
English: Qi Bo said: The color of the yin current corresponds to its meridian, while the color of the yang current is fickle. It changes with the change of the four o'clock. This is all normal and disease-free.

古文：寒多则凝泣，凝泣则青黑；热多则淖泽，淖泽则黄赤。
现代文：寒气多时则气血运行迟滞，因而多出现青黑之色；热气多时则气血运行滑利，因而多出现黄红的颜色。
English: When there is a lot of cold air, the qi and blood run sluggishly, resulting in more blue-black color; when there is a lot of hot air, the qi and blood run smoothly, resulting in more yellow-red color.

古文：五色具见者，谓之寒热。帝曰：善。
现代文：如果是五色全部显露，那就是过寒或过热所引起的变化，是疾病的表现。黄帝说：好。
English: If all five colors are revealed, it is a change caused by cold or overheating, a manifestation of the disease. Huangdi said: Good.
