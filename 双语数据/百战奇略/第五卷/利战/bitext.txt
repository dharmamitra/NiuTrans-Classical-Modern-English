古文：凡与敌战，其将愚而不知变，可诱之以利。彼贪利而不知害，可设伏兵以击之，其军可败。
现代文：凡是对敌作战，敌将愚顽而不知机变，可以用小利来引诱它上钩；敌人贪图小利而不知利害，可以设下圈套，打他埋伏，这样，敌人就可以被打败。
English: In any battle against the enemy, the enemy will be stubborn and not know how to change, and can use small profits to lure it to the hook; the enemy is greedy for small profits and does not know the interests, and can set traps and ambush him, so that the enemy can be defeated.

古文：法曰： 利而诱之。 
现代文：诚如兵法所说： 敌人贪婪好利，就用小利引诱它。 
English: As it is said in the Art of War: If the enemy is greedy and eager for profit, lure him with a small profit.

古文：春秋，楚伐绞。
现代文：春秋时期，楚国进攻绞国，军队进驻于绞城之南门。
English: In the Spring and Autumn Period, Chu attacked Zhouzhou, and the army was stationed at the south gate of Zhouzhou.

古文：莫敖屈瑕曰： 绞小而轻，轻则寡谋。
现代文：楚国的莫敖屈瑕向楚王建议说： 绞国地小而人轻佻，轻佻就缺少谋略。
English: The king of Chu was advised by the king of Chu: If the country is small and the people are frivolous, then frivolity is a lack of strategy.

古文：请无捍采樵者以诱之。 
现代文：因此，请大王派出一些不设卫兵保护的砍柴人，以此来引诱绞人就范。 
English: Therefore, the king was asked to send out some woodcutters without the protection of guards to lure the hangman into submission.

古文：从之。
现代文：楚王采纳了屈瑕的建议。
English: I followed his advice.

古文：绞获三十人。
现代文：这样，绞军便轻而易举地捕获了楚军三十名砍柴人。
English: In this way, the garrison easily captured thirty woodcutters from the Chu army.

古文：明日，绞人争出，驱楚役徒于山中。
现代文：第二天，绞军又争相出城，于山中追逐楚军砍柴人。
English: On the second day, the Zhian army also rushed out of the city, chasing the Chu army to chop wood in the mountains.

古文：楚人坐其北门，而伏诸山下，大败之。
现代文：楚军预先守候在绞城北门，并埋设伏兵于山下，结果大败绞军，与绞国签订了城下之盟而胜利回国。
English: The Chu army waited in advance at the north gate of Hengcheng, and laid an ambush at the foot of the mountain. As a result, they defeated the Hengcheng army, signed an alliance under the castle with Hengguo, and returned home in victory.
