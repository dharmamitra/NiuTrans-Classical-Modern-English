古文：欲知得失情，必审名察形。
现代文：想要懂得得失福祸的道理，就一定要审知事物的名称与客观存在之间的关系。
English: If you want to understand the principle of gain and loss, you must examine the relationship between the name of the object and its objective existence.

古文：形恒自定，是我愈静；事恒自施，是我无为。
现代文：天下万物都自有它们确定的归宿，因此人就更应该持守清静。天下万物都自有它们运行发展的规律，因此人也就应该虚静无为。
English: All things in the world have their own predetermined destinations, so it is all the more important for people to maintain tranquility. All things in the world have their own laws of operation and development, so people should also be silent and inactive.

古文：静翳不动，来自至，去自往。
现代文：人应该虚静专一不妄施为，事物的发生与消逝皆有其客观依据，要听其自便宛转顺应。
English: People should be quiet, single-minded, and not act recklessly. The occurrence and disappearance of things all have their objective basis, and we should listen to them and adapt to them.

古文：能一乎？
现代文：能做到用心专一吗？
English: Can you be unique?

古文：能止乎？
现代文：能做到持意静定吗？
English: Is it possible to stop at nothing?

古文：能毋有己，能自择而尊理乎？
现代文：在判断事物的时候，能不能以客观为依据而排除主观臆测呢？
English: When judging things, can subjective speculation be ruled out on the basis of objectivity?

古文：葆也，屯也，其如莫存。万物群至，我无不能应。
现代文：或隐或显或静定或动出，总能维持一种若有若无的超然境界。这样的话，一任事物纷至沓来，皆能应付自如。
English: Whether implicit or explicit, static or dynamic, they can always maintain a transcendental state of being. In this way, they can deal with any things that come up.

古文：我不藏故，不挟陈。
现代文：古旧的东西听其自去，新生的东西任其自来，而不要主观人为地去介入。
English: The old things should be left alone, and the new things should be left alone, instead of being subjectively and artificially interfered with.

古文：向者已去，至者乃新。
现代文：消逝的是过时的东西，来到的是新生的东西。
English: Out with the old, in with the new.

古文：新故不摎，我有所周。
现代文：天下万事万物，无论是新生的还是死灭的，都不能扰乱虚静的心灵，这是因为我们能够与物宛转、顺其自然的缘故。
English: Everything under the sun, whether newly born or dead, cannot disturb the tranquil mind, because we can relate to things and let them take their natural course.
