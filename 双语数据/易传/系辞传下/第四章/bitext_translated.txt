古文：阳卦多阴，阴卦多阳，其故何也？阳卦奇，阴卦偶。
现代文：本来阳卦适宜阳爻居多，阴卦适宜多阴爻，为何现在反而相反，阳卦多阴，阴卦多阳呢？
English: Originally, Yang Gua was most suitable for impotence, and Yin Gua was most suitable for effeminacy. Why is it now the opposite, Yang Gua is more effeminate, and Yin Gua is more masculine?

古文：其德行何也？阳一君而二民，君子之道也。
现代文：就以奇偶来说，阳卦以奇为主，例如震坎艮三卦为阳卦，都是一阳二阴，所以说，阴爻多于阳爻。
English: As far as odd and even are concerned, Yang Gua is dominated by odd, for example, Zhenkangqian and Sanga are Yang Gua, both are one yang and two yang, so Yin and Yang are more than Yang and Yang.

古文：阴二君而一民，小人之道也。
现代文：阴卦以偶数为主，如巽离兑三卦为阴卦，都是二阳一阴，所以说，阳爻多于阴爻。
English: The yin and yang are mainly even numbers. For example, the yin and yang are both two yang and one yin. Therefore, the yang is more than the yin and yang.
