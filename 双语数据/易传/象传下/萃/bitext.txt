古文：泽上于地，萃；君子以除戎器，戒不虞。乃乱乃萃，其志乱也。
现代文：《萃卦》的卦象是：坤下兑上，为地上有湖，四面八方的细流都源源不断汇入湖中之表象，象征着聚合；在这种众流会聚的时候，必然会现鱼龙混杂、泥沙俱下的情况，因此君子应当修缮甲杖兵器，以防发生意想不到的变故。
English: According to the "Tale of the Three Kingdoms," there is a lake on the earth, and streams from all directions flow into the lake, symbolizing convergence. When these streams converge, it is inevitable that there will be a mixture of fish and dragons and muddy sand. Therefore, a gentleman should repair his armor to prevent unexpected changes.

古文：引吉无咎，中未变也。往无咎，上巽也。大吉无咎，位不当也。萃有位，志未光也。赍咨涕洟，未安上也。
现代文： 各种乱子就会发生而凑到一起 ，不是由于别的原因，主要是因为内心的虔诚不能始终如一，陷于迷惑混乱所致。 引退谦让会带来吉祥，没有灾难 ，这是因为该爻位置居中而适当，当会聚的时候，它既不偏不激也不过于保守，虔诚地遵循中庸之道始终不曾改变，因而能够谦让而逢凶化吉。 前去行事不会遇到灾祸 这是因为遇到居于上位的阳刚气十足者。
English: Disturbances of all kinds will occur and come together, not for any other reason, but chiefly because the devoutness of the heart is not consistent and is caught up in confusion.Retiring and yielding will bring good fortune, not disaster, this is because the yakitori is in the middle and appropriate, when meeting, it is neither unbalanced nor too conservative, devoutly following the middle way and has never changed, so it can yield and meet adversity.Going forward will not encounter disaster This is because it encounters a manly man who is in a higher position.
