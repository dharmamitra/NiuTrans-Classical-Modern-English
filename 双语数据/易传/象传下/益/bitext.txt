现代文：《益卦》的卦象是震下巽上，为狂风和惊雷互相激荡，相得益彰之表象，象征 增益 的意思；从中得到的启示就是：君子应当看到良好的行为就马上向它看齐，有了过错就马上改正，不断增强自身的美好品德。
English: "Yi Gua"'s Gua is like a tremor on the tremor, for the stormy wind and thunderstorms stir each other, the appearance of mutual benefit, symbolizing the meaning of "gain"; the revelation from it is: a gentleman should see a good behavior and immediately align with it, and if there is a mistake, immediately correct it, and continuously enhance his own good character.

English: If you can get great luck, you will not be blamed, indicating that when you are doing a great job, you must be cautious and careful, try not to be too extravagant and extravagant, and not to make the people work too hard.

古文：惠我德，大得志也。莫益之，偏辞也。或击之，自外来也。
现代文：将得到的好处用来解救他人的危难之事，是保全自身的最好办法。
English: The best way to protect yourself is to use your winnings to save others from danger.
