古文：山上有雷，小过；君子以行过乎恭，丧过乎哀，用过乎俭。飞鸟以凶，不可如何也。
现代文：《小过卦》的卦象是艮下震上，为山上响雷之表象，雷声超过了寻常的雷鸣，以此比喻 小有过越 ，君子应效法 小过 之象，在一些寻常小事上能略有过分，如行止时过分恭敬，遇到丧事时过分悲哀，日常用度过分节俭，为的是矫枉过正。
English: The gaga of "Little Excesses" is like a tremor under the armpit, and is the appearance of thunder on the mountain. The thunder exceeds the ordinary thunder. As a metaphor, the small exceeds the ordinary, and the gentleman should follow the example of the small excesses. In some ordinary trifles, it can be slightly excessive, such as excessive respect when walking, excessive sorrow when encountering a funeral, and excessive thrift in daily use, in order to correct the excessive.
