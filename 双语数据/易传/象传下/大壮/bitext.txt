古文：雷在天上，大壮；君子以非礼弗履。
现代文：《大壮卦》的卦象是乾下震上，为震雷响彻天上之表象，象征着十分强盛。
English: "Dazanggua" is like a dry earthquake on the surface of the thunder and lightning, symbolizing a very strong earthquake.

古文：壮于趾，其孚穷也。
现代文：君子应该严格要求自己，不要越出准则和规律去做非分之事。
English: A gentleman should be strict with himself and not go beyond the norms and rules to do something inappropriate.

古文：九二贞吉，以中也。小人用壮，君子罔也。藩决不羸，尚往也。
现代文：而 忍耐坚持，承受艰难困苦而不被压垮，就会吉祥 ，说明，只要能够坚持忍耐，就一定会渡过难关。
English: And perseverance, enduring hardships without being overwhelmed, will be auspicious, indicating that as long as you can persevere, you will definitely get through the difficulties.
