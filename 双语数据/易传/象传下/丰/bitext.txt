古文：雷电皆至，丰；君子以折狱致刑。虽旬无咎，过旬灾也。
现代文：《丰卦》的卦象是离下雷上，离又代表闪电，震为雷，为雷电同时到来之表象，象征着盛大丰满；君子应该像雷电那样，审案用刑正大光明。
English: "Feng Gua"'s Gua is like stepping on a lightning bolt, and the lightning bolt represents lightning, and the shock is thunder, which is the appearance of lightning coming at the same time, symbolizing a grand fullness; a gentleman should be like lightning, and the trial and punishment should be very bright.

古文：有孚发若，信以发志也。丰其沛，不可大事也。折其右肱，终不可用也。丰其蔀，位不当也。日中见斗，幽不明也。遇其夷主，吉行也。
现代文： 虽然合作十天也不致受害 ，但是过了十天就可能会有灾祸。 能以自己的至诚之心去启迪 ，是有信以展拓其盛大之志。 光明被云遮掩 ，不可成就胜任大事； 右臂折断而无为慎守 ，最终得不到重用或不可能有所作用了。 光明遭到云的蒙蔽 ，是说九四爻所居的爻位不当； 明亮的白天却看到了夜晚的北斗星 ，说明由于蒙蔽而出现昏暗； 但若遇到明主赏识 ，行动还是会获得吉祥的。
English: Although cooperation will not be harmed for ten days, there may be a disaster after ten days. It is faith that can inspire with its sincere heart to expand its grand aspirations. The light is obscured by the cloud, it can not achieve the competent big things; the right arm is broken without being cautious, and eventually it can not be reused or it can not be effective. The light is blinded by the cloud, which means that the position occupied by the Nine-Four Immortals is inappropriate; the bright day sees the night's Big Dipper, indicating that it is darkened due to the blinding; but if it encounters the recognition of the Lord, the action will still be auspicious.

古文：六五之吉，有庆也。丰其屋，天际翔也。窥其户阒其无人，自藏也。
现代文：《丰卦》的第五爻位的吉祥，必定会有喜庆。 高大的房屋 ，居内自蔽，孤立于人，好似在天际飞翔； 窥视窗户，寂静而无人 ，深深隐藏踪迹。
English: The auspiciousness of the fifth rank of "Fenghua" will surely have a celebration. Tall houses, self-concealed inside, isolated from people, seem to fly in the sky; peeking through windows, silent and unattended, deeply hidden traces.
