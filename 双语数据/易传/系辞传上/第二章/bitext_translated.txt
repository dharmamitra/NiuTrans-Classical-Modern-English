古文：六爻之动，三极之道也。
现代文：六爻的动态，就是天地人才的道理。
English: The dynamic of the Six Dynasties is the reason for the talent of heaven and earth.

古文：是故君子所居而安者，《易》之序也；所乐而玩者，爻之辞也。
现代文：所以君子平居之时，能心安理得，这是因为能法象《易经》的文辞呀。仔细揣摩，慢慢玩味每爻之辞。
English: Therefore, when a gentleman is at peace, he can be at peace, because he can imitate the words of the "I Ching." Carefully calculate and slowly play with each phrase.

古文：是故君子居则观其象而玩其辞，动则观其变而玩其占，是以自天佑之，吉无不利。
现代文：有得于心，所以乐趣无穷。所以君子平居之时就观察易象而探索玩味它的文辞；一有行动，则观察《易经》的变化，而玩味占筮的吉凶。所以能如大有卦上九爻辞所说： 从上天祐助之，完全的吉而没有不利的。 
English: Therefore, when a gentleman is at peace, he observes the I Ching and explores the words that play with it; when there is an action, he observes the changes in the I Ching, and the playfulness accounts for the fortune and misfortune. Therefore, it can be said, as in the Ninth Chapter of the Tao Te Ching: With the help from heaven, it is completely auspicious and there is no disadvantage.
