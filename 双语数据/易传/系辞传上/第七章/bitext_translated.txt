古文：子曰：《易》，其至矣乎！夫《易》，圣人所以崇德而广业也。
现代文：孔子说：《易经》的道理，是最伟大的呀，《易经》正是圣人用以崇高道德、广大事业的呀。
English: Confucius said: "The principles of the I Ching are the greatest. The I Ching is exactly what sages use for their lofty morals and vast undertakings."

古文：知崇礼卑，崇效天，卑法地。
现代文：知慧要求到崇高而后止，礼节则自谦卑入手，崇高效法天道，谦卑效法地道。
English: Wisdom requires the sublime and then stops, while etiquette starts with humility. The sublime is the way of heaven, and the humble is the way of earth.

古文：天地设位，而《易》行乎其中矣。
现代文：天地既设位，《易经》之道也就行于天地之间了。
English: As long as heaven and earth are in place, the path of the I Ching will be carried out between heaven and earth.

古文：成性存存，道义之门。
现代文：成就此崇高广大的善性，当不停地蕴存之，存养之，这就是道义所由产生的门户了。
English: The accomplishment of this lofty, expansive goodness, which should be constantly contained and nurtured, is the gateway through which morality arises.
