古文：屯，刚柔始交而难生，动乎险中，大亨贞。
现代文：屯，刚柔始相交而难以生成，动于险难之中。
English: Tun, gong and jiao are difficult to generate when they intersect, and they move in the midst of danger.

古文：雷雨之动满盈，天造草昧，宜建侯而不宁。
现代文：盛大 亨通而守正 ，雷雨震动充满，天始造化，万物萌发，适宜于封建诸侯，但将不安宁。
English: Grand, prosperous and upright, full of thunderstorms and tremors, created by nature, everything sprouting, suitable for feudal lords, but will be restless.
