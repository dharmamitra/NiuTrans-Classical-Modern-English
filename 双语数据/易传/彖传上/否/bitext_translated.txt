古文：否之匪人，不利君子贞，大往小来。
现代文： 闭塞阻隔的不是其人，不利君子，大往小来 。
English: It is not the person who obstructs the obstruction, it is the gentleman who is unfavorable, from big to small.

古文：则是天地不交而万物不通也，上下不交而天下无邦也。
现代文：则是天地之气不能互相交感而万物阻隔，上下不相交感而天下没有邦国。
English: On the other hand, the spirit of heaven and earth cannot sympathize with each other and everything is blocked, the upper and lower do not sympathize with each other and there is no state in the world.

古文：内阴而外阳，内柔而外刚，内小人而外君子。
现代文：内阴柔，而外阳刚；内柔顺，而外刚健；内为小人，而外为君子。
English: Feminine and gentle on the inside, but masculine on the outside; gentle and gentle on the inside, but tough and robust on the outside; a villain on the inside, but a gentleman on the outside.

古文：小人道长，君子道消也。
现代文：小人之道盛长，君子之道消退。
English: The way of the scoundrel prospers, but the way of the gentleman declines.
