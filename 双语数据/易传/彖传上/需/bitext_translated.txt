古文：需，须也，险在前也，刚健而不陷，其义不困穷矣。
现代文：需，等待。危险在前方，有刚健而不会陷入，其义为不困穷。
English: Need, wait. Danger is ahead, there is robustness and will not fall into, its meaning is not poor.

古文：需有孚，光亨贞吉，位乎天位，以正中也。利涉大川，往有功也。
现代文：需， 有诚信广大亨通，而占问吉利 ，位于天子之位，故居正而得中道。 宜于涉越大河 ，前往可以建功立业。
English: It is necessary to have good faith to succeed, and it is auspicious to be in the position of the son of heaven, and the former residence is right in the middle. It is suitable for crossing the river, and you can build a career.
