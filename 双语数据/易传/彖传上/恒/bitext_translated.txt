古文：恒，久也。
现代文：恒，恒久。
English: Permanently, permanently.

古文：刚上而柔下，雷风相与，巽而动，刚柔皆应，恒。
现代文：阳刚处上而阴柔处下，雷风相交与，巽顺而动，阳刚与阴柔皆相互应，故为恒。
English: The masculine is at the top and the feminine is at the bottom, the thunderstorm intersects with, and the thunderbolt moves along, the masculine and the feminine both correspond to each other, so they are constants.

古文：恒亨无咎利贞，久于其道也。
现代文：恒， 亨通，无咎，利于守正 。长久恒守其道呵！
English: Persistence, success, no blame, is conducive to keeping the right. Long-term adherence to its path!

古文：天地之道，恒久而不已也。利有攸往，终则有始也。
现代文：天地之道，恒久而不止。 利一有所往 ，终结则必有新的开始。
English: The way of heaven and earth is permanent and never ending. If there is a past, there must be a new beginning.

古文：日月得天，而能久照。四时变化，而能久成。圣人久于其道，而天下化成。
现代文：日月得天才能长久地照耀，四季交替变化才能长久地运行，圣人能长久地恒守其道，天下之才能化育而成。
English: The sun and the moon can shine for a long time, the alternating changes of the four seasons can run for a long time, the saints can keep their way for a long time, and the talent of the world can be nurtured.

古文：观其所恒，而天地万物之情可见矣。
现代文：观察其所恒守者，天地万物的情状就可以显现了！
English: By observing those who adhere to it, the state of heaven and earth and all things can be revealed!
