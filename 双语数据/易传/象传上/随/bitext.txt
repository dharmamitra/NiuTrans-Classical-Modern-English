古文：泽中有雷，随；君子以向晦入宴息。
现代文：《随卦》的卦象是震下兑上，为泽中有雷之表象。泽中有雷声，泽随从雷声而震动，这便象征随从。
English: "Suigua" is like the tremors of the earthquake, and the appearance of thunder in the lake. There is thunder in the lake, and the lake vibrates with the thunder, which is a symbol of following.

古文：官有渝，从正吉也。
现代文：君子行事要遵从合适的作息时间。
English: A gentleman should keep to a proper schedule.

古文：出门交有功，不失也。
现代文：白天出处辛劳工作，夜晚就回家睡觉安息。
English: They work hard during the day and go home at night to sleep and rest.
