古文：雷在地中，复；先王以至日闭关，商旅不行，后不省方。
现代文：《复卦》的卦象是震下坤上，为雷在地中、阳气微弱地活动之表象，因而象征复归；从前的君主在阳气初生的冬至这一天关闭关口，使商人旅客停止活动，不外出经商、旅行，君主自己也不巡行视察四方。
English: "Fu Gua"'s Gua is like a tremor on the Kun, the appearance of thunder in the earth and the weak activity of yang qi, thus symbolizing the return; the former monarch closed the pass on the day of the winter solstice when yang qi was born, so that merchants and travelers stopped their activities and did not go out to do business or travel, and the monarch himself did not patrol and inspect the four directions.

古文：不远之复，以修身也。休复之吉，以下仁也。频复之厉，义无咎也。中行独复，以从道也。敦复无悔，中以自考也。迷复之凶，反君道也。

If you repeatedly make mistakes, you can repeatedly correct mistakes and return to the right path, so although there is danger, you can still reform and return to the right path, so you will not encounter disaster in the end.
