古文：天在山中，大畜；君子以多识前贤往行，以畜其德。有厉利已，不犯灾也。舆说輹，中无尤也。
现代文：《大畜卦》的第四爻位的 大吉大利 ，是因为能够防患于未然，未雨而绸缪，因而是可喜的。 《大畜卦》的第五爻位的吉祥，是因为能够抓住事物的关键，从根本上予以治理，因而是可庆可贺的。 四通八达，多么畅通无阻的天街大道 ，是说由于大量畜养积聚贤士，天下已经贤路大开了。
English: The fourth-ranked great good luck of "Animal Husbandry" is gratifying because it can prevent trouble in advance and work hard before it rains; the fifth-ranked auspiciousness of "Animal Husbandry" is gratifying because it can grasp the key to things and fundamentally govern them; the smooth and unimpeded Tianjie Boulevard is said to have opened up the world to sages due to the large number of livestock and the accumulation of sages.
