古文：萃，聚也。
现代文：萃，聚。
English: Gather, gather, gather.

古文：顺以说，刚中而应，故聚也。王假有庙，致孝享也。利见大人亨，聚以正也。用大牲吉，利有攸往，顺天命也。
现代文：顺从而招喜悦，阳刚居中而相应，故为聚。 大王至宗庙 ，致孝祖之祭。 利于见有权势的人，亨通 ，聚集必以正道。 用大的牲畜吉利，利有所往 ，顺从天命。
English: Conformity to it brings joy, masculinity is in the middle and corresponding, so it is a gathering. The great king to the ancestral temple, to the sacrifice of the filial ancestor. It is good to see the powerful people, the successful, the gathering must be the right way. Use large livestock for good luck, the profit has the past, obey the fate of heaven.

古文：观其所聚，而天地万物之情可见矣。
现代文：观察它感应到的事情，天地万物的情状就可以知道了。
English: The state of all things in heaven and earth can be known by observing the things it senses.
