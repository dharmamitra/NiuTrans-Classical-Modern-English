古文：节亨，刚柔分而刚得中。苦节不可贞，其道穷也。
现代文：节， 亨通 ，阳刚阴柔分居，而阳刚得中。 苦苦节制不可以守正 ，节制之道穷困。
English: Section, prosperity, masculine and feminine softly separated, and masculine and feminine get in the middle. Hardship and restraint can not be kept, the way of restraint is poverty.

古文：说以行险，当位以节，中正以通。
现代文：喜悦以行险阻，当位而施以节制，中正通达。
English: Joy is to take risks and obstacles, to be in place and exercise restraint, and to be in the middle.

古文：天地节，而四时成。
现代文：天地节制，而四时的变化才形成。
English: Heaven and earth are restrained, and the four o'clock change is formed.

古文：节以制度，不伤财，不害民。
现代文：以制度节制，不损伤财物，不妨害民众。
English: With institutional restraint, do not damage property, do not harm the public.
