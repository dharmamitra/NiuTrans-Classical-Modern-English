古文：韩公叔与几瑟争国，中庶子谓太子曰： 不若及齐师未入，急击公叔。 
现代文：韩公叔帮助公子咎与几瑟争夺国权。中庶子郑强对几瑟说， 不如趁齐国军队还没有攻打进来，赶快除掉公叔。 
English: Uncle Han helped Guan Guan and Guise to fight for state power. The middle son, Zheng Qiang, said to Guise that it would be better to take advantage of the fact that the Qi army had not yet attacked and quickly get rid of Uncle Guan.

古文：太子曰： 不可。
现代文：几瑟说： 不可。
English: Kayser said: No.

古文：战之于国中必分。 
现代文：在国内打内战，国家必然会分裂。 
English: If there is a civil war at home, the country will inevitably split.

古文：对曰： 事不成，身必危，尚何足以图国之全为？ 
现代文：郑强回答说： 这件事不成功，您自身必然会遭到危险，还谈什么考虑国家的完整呢？ 
English: Zheng Qiang replied: If this matter does not succeed, you yourself will inevitably be in danger. What is there to say about considering the integrity of the country?

古文：太子甫听，齐师果耕畜，太子出走。
现代文：几瑟不听，齐国军队果然侵入韩国，几瑟被迫逃出韩国。
English: Qise did not listen, and the Qi army did invade South Korea. Qise was forced to flee South Korea.
