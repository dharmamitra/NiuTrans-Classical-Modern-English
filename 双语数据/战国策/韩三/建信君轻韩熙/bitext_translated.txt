古文：建信君轻韩熙，赵敖为谓建信侯曰： 国形有之而存，无之而亡者，魏也。
现代文：建信君蔑视韩熙，赵敖为他对建信君说： 从国家形势看，与邻国联合就能生存，不与邻国联合就会灭亡，是魏国。
English: Jianxinjun disdained Han Xi, Zhao Wei said to Jianxinjun for him: From the perspective of the national situation, if we unite with our neighbors, we can survive, but if we do not unite with our neighbors, we will perish. This is Wei.

古文：不可无而从者，韩也。
现代文：不能舍弃邻国而进行合纵的，是韩国。
English: It is South Korea that cannot forsake its neighbors and pursue unification.

古文：今君之轻韩熙者，交善楚、魏也。
现代文：如今您轻视韩熙的原因，是想同楚、魏两国建立良好的邦交。
English: The reason why you now despise Han Xi is that you want to establish good diplomatic relations with Chu and Wei.

古文：秦见君之交反善于楚、魏也，其收韩必重矣。
现代文：秦国看到您反而与楚、魏两国交往密切，秦国一定会更加重视拉拢韩国。
English: On the contrary, Qin sees that you have close exchanges with Chu and Wei, and Qin will certainly pay more attention to attracting South Korea.

古文：从则韩轻，横则韩重，则无从轻矣。
现代文：如果实行合纵韩国就受到轻视，实行连横韩国就得到重视，那么赵国却不存在合纵，就受到轻视的问题。
English: If the unification of Korea is despised, and the unification of Korea is valued, then the unification of Zhao does not exist, and it is despised.

古文：秦出兵于三川，则南围鄢，蔡、邵之道不通矣。
现代文：秦国如果从三川出兵，就会向南围困鄢陵，上蔡、召陵的道路就不通了。
English: If Qin dispatched troops from Sanchuan, they would besiege Fuling to the south, and the road to Shangcai and Zhaoling would be impassable.

古文：魏急，其救赵必缓矣。
现代文：魏国形势危急，诸侯们援救赵国的行动就会延缓进行。
English: The situation in Wei was critical, and the action of the princes to rescue Zhao would be delayed.

古文：秦举兵破邯郸，赵必亡矣。
现代文：泰国发兵攻破邯郸，越国必然会灭亡。
English: If Thailand sends troops to break the shackles, Vietnam will inevitably perish.

古文：故君收韩，可以无亹。
现代文：所以您只要得到韩国的支持，就可以避免出现漏洞。 
English: So as long as you have the support of South Korea, you can avoid loopholes.
