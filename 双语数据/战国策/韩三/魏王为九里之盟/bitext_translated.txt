古文：魏王为九里之盟，且复天子。
现代文：魏王主持诸侯在逢泽会盟，准备重新恢复天子权威。
English: The Wei king presided over the princes' alliance in Huazhe and prepared to restore the authority of the Son of Heaven.

古文：房喜谓韩王曰： 勿听之也，大国恶有天子，而小国利之。
现代文：房喜对韩王说： 不要听他们的，大国厌恶天子的存在，而小国却认为天子的存在对自己有利。
English: Fang Xi said to King Han: Don't listen to them. Big countries hate the existence of the Son of Heaven, while small countries think that the existence of the Son of Heaven is beneficial to them.

古文：王与大国弗听，魏安能与小国立之。 
现代文：大王和其它大国不听从他们，魏国又怎能与一些小国复立天子的权威。 
English: If the great king and other great powers did not listen to them, how could Wei and some small countries restore the authority of the Son of Heaven.
