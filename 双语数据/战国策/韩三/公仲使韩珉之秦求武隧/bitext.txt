古文：公仲使韩珉之秦求武隧，而恐楚之怒也。
现代文：公仲朋派韩珉去秦国要武隧，又担心楚国恼怒。
English: Gongzhong and his friends sent Han Xuan to Qin to demand the tunnel, fearing the wrath of Chu.

古文：唐客谓公仲曰： 韩之事秦也，且以求武隧也，非弊邑之所憎也。
现代文：唐客对公仲说： 韩国侍奉秦国，是准备要回武隧，这不是敝国所憎恨的。
English: Tang guests said to Gongzhong: South Korea served the Qin State and is preparing to return to Wuchang Tunnel. This is not what China hates.

古文：韩已得武隧，其形乃可以善楚。
现代文：韩国得到武隧后，那样的形势下才可以亲近楚国。
English: After South Korea obtained the Wuchang Tunnel, it was only under such circumstances that it was possible to get close to Chu.

古文：臣愿有言，而不敢为楚计。
现代文：我愿意说几句，并不敢为楚国打算。
English: I would like to say a few words, but I dare not do so for the sake of Chu.

古文：今韩之父兄得众者毋相。韩不能独立，势必不善楚。
现代文：观在韩国的父兄，得到众人支持的没有做相国，韩国不能独立，势必不会亲近楚国。
English: Looking at the father and brother in South Korea, if they do not have the support of the people to become a sister country, South Korea cannot be independent and will not be close to Chu.

古文：王曰： 吾欲以国辅韩珉而相之可乎?
现代文：楚王曾说： 我想用全国的力量帮助韩珉做相国，可以吗？
English: King Chu once said: I want to use the power of the whole country to help Han Yu become a sister country, is that okay?

古文：父兄恶珉，珉必以国保楚。 
现代文：韩国父兄厌恶韩珉，韩珉一定会让韩国维护楚国。 
English: Korean parents and brothers detest Han Kuo, Han Kuo will definitely let South Korea defend Chu.

古文：公仲说。士唐客于诸公，而使之主韩、楚之事。
现代文：公仲朋听了很高兴，就向大臣们推荐唐客做官，让他掌管韩、楚两国之间的事务。
English: He was very pleased to hear this, and recommended Tang Ke to the ministers as an official to be in charge of the affairs between Korea and Chu.
