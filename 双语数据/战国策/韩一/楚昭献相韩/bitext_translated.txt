古文：楚昭献相韩。
现代文：楚国的昭献在韩国做相国。
English: The Zhaokeng of the State of Chu is a sister state in South Korea.

古文：秦且攻韩，韩废昭献。
现代文：秦国将要进攻韩国，韩国罢免了昭献。
English: Qin is about to attack South Korea, and South Korea has dismissed Zhaochen.

古文：昭献令人谓公叔曰： 不如贵昭献以固楚，秦必曰楚、韩合矣。 
现代文：昭献派人对韩国的公叔说： 不如使昭献的地位更尊贵来加强同楚国的联盟，秦王一定会说，楚国、韩国已经联合在一起了。 
English: Zhaokeng sent a message to his uncle in Korea: "It would be better to make Zhaokeng's position more honorable to strengthen the alliance with Chu. The Qin King will surely say that Chu and Korea have already united.
