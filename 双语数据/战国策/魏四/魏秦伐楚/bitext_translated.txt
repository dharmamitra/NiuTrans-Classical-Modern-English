古文：魏、秦伐楚，魏王不欲。
现代文：魏国、秦国一同攻打楚国，魏王不想参战。
English: Wei and Qin attacked Chu together, but the Wei king did not want to go to war.

古文：楼缓谓魏王曰： 王不与秦攻楚，楚且与秦攻王。
现代文：楼缓对魏王说： 大王不同秦国攻打楚国，楚国就会和秦国攻打大王。
English: Lou Ye said to King Wei: If the Great King doesn't like Qin attacking Chu, then Chu will attack the Great King together with Qin.

古文：王不如令秦、楚战，王交制之也。 
现代文：大王不如让秦国、楚国交战，大王同时牵制它们。 
English: It would be better for the great king to let the Qin and Chu kingdoms fight, and at the same time restrain them.
