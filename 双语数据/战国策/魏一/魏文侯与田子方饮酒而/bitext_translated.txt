古文：魏文侯与田子方饮酒而称乐。
现代文：魏文侯和田子方一起饮酒谈论音乐。
English: Wei Wenhou and Tian Zifang drink together and talk about music.

古文：文侯曰： 钟声不比乎，左高。 
现代文：魏文侯说： 钟声不协调了吧？左面的声音高。 
English: Wei Wenhou said: The bells are not coordinated, are they? The sound on the left is high.

古文：田子方笑。
现代文：田子方笑了起来。
English: Ms. Tago started laughing.

古文：文侯曰： 奚笑? 
现代文：魏文侯说： 你笑什么？ 
English: Wei Wenhou said: What are you laughing at?

古文：子方曰： 臣闻之，君明则乐官，不明则乐音。
现代文：田子方说： 臣下听说，做国君的明理就喜欢治官之道；不明理就偏爱音乐。
English: Tashikata said, "My vassals have heard that when you are the sovereign, you like the way of governing the country for obvious reasons, but prefer music for unclear reasons.

古文：今君审于声，臣恐君之聋于官也。 文侯曰： 善，敬闻命。 
现代文：现在您对音乐辨别得很清楚，臣下唯恐您在治官方面有些聋。 魏文侯说： 对，敬听您的教诲。 
English: Now that you can clearly distinguish music, your subjects are afraid that you are a little deaf when it comes to governing officials. Wei Wenhou said: Yes, listen to your teachings.
