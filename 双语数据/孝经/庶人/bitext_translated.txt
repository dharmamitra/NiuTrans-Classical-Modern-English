古文：用天之道，分地之利，谨身节用，以养父母，此庶人之孝也。
现代文：利用自然的季节，认清土地的高下优劣，行为谨慎，节省俭约，以此来孝养父母，这就是普通老百姓的孝道了。
English: Taking advantage of the natural seasons, recognizing the merits and demerits of the land, acting prudently, and being frugal, in order to be filial to parents, this is the filial piety of ordinary people.

古文：故自天子至于庶人，孝无终始，而患不及者，未之有也。
现代文：所以上自天子，下至普通老百姓，不论尊卑高下，孝道是无始无终，永恒存在的，有人担心自己不能做到孝，那是没有的事情。
English: Therefore, from the Son of Heaven to ordinary people, no matter how high or low they are, filial piety is beginningless, endless, and eternal. Some people worry that they cannot be filial, but that is not the case.
