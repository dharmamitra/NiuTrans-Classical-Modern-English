古文：淳于髡曰： 男女授受不亲，礼与？ 
现代文：淳于髡说： 男女之间不亲手传递接受东西，这是礼制吗？ 
English: Jun Yuxuan said, "Is it proper etiquette for men and women not to pass and receive things with their own hands?

古文：孟子曰： 礼也。 
现代文：孟子说： 是礼制。 
English: Mencius said: It is the etiquette.

古文：曰： 嫂溺则援之以手乎？ 
现代文：淳于髡说： 如果嫂嫂掉入水中，要伸手去救她吗？ 
English: Jun Yuxuan said, "If your sister-in-law fell into the water, would you reach out to save her?

古文：曰： 嫂溺不援，是豺狼也。
现代文：孟子说： 嫂嫂淹入水中不伸手去救，简直就是豺狼。
English: Mencius said: If a sister-in-law falls into the water and does not reach out to save her, she is nothing but a wolf.

古文：男女授受不亲，礼也；嫂溺援之以手者，权也。 
现代文：所谓男女授受不亲，是礼制；嫂嫂淹入水中，伸手去救，是一种权宜变通之计。 
English: The so-called giving and receiving of men and women is a form of etiquette; a sister-in-law drowning in the water and reaching out for help is an expedient workaround.

古文：曰： 今天下溺矣，夫子之不援，何也？ 
现代文：淳于髡说： 如今天下百姓都淹入水中，先生却不伸手去救援，这是为什么呢？ 
English: Chun Yuxuan said, "If all the people are drowning today, but you don't reach out to rescue them, why is that?

古文：曰： 天下溺，援之以道；嫂溺，援之以手。
现代文：孟子说： 天下百姓都淹入水中，要想救援，就要有一定的方法。
English: Mencius said: All the people in the world are drowning in water. If you want to rescue them, you must have a certain method.

古文：子欲手援天下乎？ 
现代文：你想让我用一只手去救援天下百姓吗？ 
English: Do you want me to save the people of the world with one hand?
