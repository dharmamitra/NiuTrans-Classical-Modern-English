古文：孟子曰： 爱人不亲反其仁，治人不治反其智，礼人不答反其敬。
现代文：孟子说： 爱别人别人却不亲近自己，那就应该反问自己的仁爱是否够；管理别人别人却不服管理，那就应反问自己的管理才智是否有问题；待人礼貌别人却不回应，那就应反问自己的礼貌是否到家。
English: Mencius said: If you love others but are not close to yourself, then you should ask yourself whether your benevolence is enough; if you manage others but do not accept management, then you should ask yourself whether your management ability is a problem; if you are polite to others but do not respond, then you should ask yourself whether your politeness has reached home.

古文：行有不得者，皆反求诸己，其身正而天下归之。
现代文：凡是行为得不到预期的效果，都应该从自身寻找原因，自身行为端正了，天下的人自然就会归服。
English: If one's actions do not have the expected effect, one should look for the reasons from oneself. If one's actions are correct, people around the world will naturally submit.

古文：诗云： 永言配命，自求多福。  
现代文：《诗经》说： 常思虑自己的行为是否合乎天理，以求美好的幸福生活  
English: The Book of Songs says: "Always consider whether your actions are in accordance with the principles of heaven, so as to live a good and happy life."
