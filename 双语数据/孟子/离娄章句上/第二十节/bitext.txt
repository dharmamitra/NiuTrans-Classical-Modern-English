古文：孟子曰： 人不足与适也，政不足间也。
现代文：孟子说： 那些当权的小人不值得去指责，他们的政事也不值得去非议。
English: Mencius said: Those petty people in power are not worth criticizing, and their political affairs are not worth criticizing.

古文：惟大人为能格君心之非。
现代文：惟有那些高智慧的大人能纠正国君思想上的错误。
English: Only those highly intelligent adults can correct the mistakes in the thoughts of the sovereign.

古文：君仁莫不仁，君义莫不义，君正莫不正。
现代文：国君仁，便没有人不仁；国君义，便没有人不义；国君正，便没有人不正。
English: When the sovereign is kind, no one is unkind; when the sovereign is righteous, no one is unrighteous; when the sovereign is just, no one is unjust.

古文：一正君而国定矣。 
现代文：一旦端正了国君，国家便安定了。 
English: Once the sovereign is corrected, the country will be stable.
