古文：孟子曰： 万物皆备于我矣。反身而诚，乐莫大焉。
现代文：孟子说： 万物之情我都具备了，反身而自求诚意，快乐没有比这个更大的了。
English: Mencius said: I have all the feelings of all things, and I seek sincerity reflexively. There is no greater happiness than this.

古文：强恕而行，求仁莫近焉。 
现代文：努力不懈地以推己及人的恕道去行事，寻求仁的道路没有比这个更近的了。 
English: There is no nearer way to seek benevolence than to strive ceaselessly to act in the way of self-advancement and the forgiveness of others.
