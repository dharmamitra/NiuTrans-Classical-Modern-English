古文：孟子曰： 仁，人心也；义，人路也。舍其路而弗由，放其心而不知求，哀哉！
现代文：孟子说： 与人相互亲爱，是人的本心；义，是人的正路；舍弃正道不走，放弃本心而不知求回，太可悲了！
English: Mencius said: To love each other is the true heart of man; to be righteous is the right path for man; to abandon the true path and not follow it, to give up one's original heart without knowing how to return, is too sad!

古文：人有鸡犬放，则知求之；有放心，而不知求。
现代文：人们的鸡犬丢失了，尚且知道寻求回来；可是丢失了良心却不知道寻求回来。
English: People's chickens and dogs are lost, yet they know how to seek to return; but they have lost their conscience, yet they do not know how to seek to return.

古文：学问之道无他，求其放心而已矣。 
现代文：学和问的道路没有别的，只是寻求回丢弃的本心而已。 
English: There is no other way to learn and ask, but to seek to return to the discarded original mind.
