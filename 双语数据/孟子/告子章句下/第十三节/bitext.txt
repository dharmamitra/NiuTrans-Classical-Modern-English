古文：夫苟好善，则四海之内，皆将轻千里而来告之以善。
现代文：一个人如果喜好善良，那么四海之内的人们都会不远千里赶来把善告诉他。
English: If a person likes goodness, then people from all over the world will come thousands of miles to tell him about goodness.

古文：夫苟不好善，则人将曰： 訑訑，予既已知之矣。

English: If a person does not like kindness, then people will say: Look at his smug look, I already know this kind of person.

古文：士止于千里之外，则谗谄面谀之人至矣。

English: If the readers are thousands of miles away, then the flatterers will come.

古文：与谗谄面谀之人居，国欲治，可得乎？
