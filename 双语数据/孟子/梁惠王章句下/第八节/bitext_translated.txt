古文：齐宣王问曰： 汤放桀，武王伐纣，有诸？ 
现代文：齐宣王问孟子： 商汤流放夏桀，武王讨伐商纣，有这些事吗？ 
English: Qixuan Wang asked Mencius: Shang Tang exiled Xia Yao, Wu Wang fought Shang Yao, is there such a thing?

古文：孟子对曰： 于传有之。 
现代文：孟子回答道： 文献上有这样的记载。 
English: Mencius replied: It is so recorded in the literature.

古文：曰： 臣弑其君，可乎？ 
现代文：宣王问： 臣子杀掉他的君主，可以吗？ 
English: Xuan Wang asked: Is it okay for a subject to kill his sovereign?

古文：曰： 贼仁者谓之贼，贼义者谓之残，残贼之人谓之一夫。
现代文：孟子说： 败坏仁的人叫贼，败坏义的人叫残；残、贼这样的人叫独夫。
English: Mencius said: A person who corrupts kindness is called a thief; a person who corrupts righteousness is called a ruffian; a person who is both a ruffian and a thief is called a loner.

古文：闻诛一夫纣矣，未闻弑君也。 
现代文：我只听说周武王杀了独夫商纣，没听说臣杀君。
English: I've only heard that King Zhou Wu killed his sole trader, but I haven't heard that his vassal killed him.
