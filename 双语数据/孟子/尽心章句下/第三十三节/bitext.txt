古文：孟子曰： 尧舜，性者也；汤武，反之也。
现代文：孟子说： 尧和舜，是本性的人；商汤王和武王，则是返回本性的人。
English: Mencius said: Zou and Zou are people of nature; Shang Tang Wang and Wu Wang are people who return to nature.

古文：动容周旋中礼者，盛德之至也；哭死而哀，非为生者也；经德不回，非以干禄也；言语必信，非以正行也。
现代文：举动、容貌都合于礼，是美德中的极点。哭死者而悲哀，不是做给生者看的。按道德行事而不违背，不是为了谋求官位。言谈语言必然诚信，并不是为了端正行为。
English: Behavior and appearance are suitable for etiquette and are the pinnacle of virtue. Weeping for the dead is not for the living. Acting in accordance with morality without violating it is not for the purpose of seeking a position. Speaking the language is necessarily honest, not for the purpose of correct behavior.

古文：君子行法，以俟命而已矣。 
现代文：君子行为于法度，只是遵守命运的安排而已。 
English: A gentleman acts according to the law, but only obeys the arrangement of fate.
