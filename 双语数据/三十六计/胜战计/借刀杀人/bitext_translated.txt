古文：敌已明，友未定，引友杀敌，不自出力，以《损》推演。
现代文：敌人的情况已经明了，盟友的态度还在犹豫，这时应极力设法诱使盟友去攻打敌人，来保存自己的实力。这是从《周易·损》卦推演出的计策。
English: When the enemy's situation is already clear and the attitude of the ally is still hesitant, it is necessary to try hard to induce the ally to attack the enemy to preserve its strength. This is a strategy deduced from "Zhou Yi · Loss" gua.
