古文：存其形，完其势；友不疑，敌不动。巽而止蛊。
现代文：保存阵地已有的战斗形貌，造成强大的声势，使友军不怀疑，敌人也不敢贸然进犯，而我却可以隐蔽地抽走精锐部队去袭击别处的敌军。
English: Preserve the existing battle configuration of the position, create a strong momentum, so that the friendly army does not doubt, and the enemy does not dare to attack rashly, but I can secretly take away the elite troops to attack the enemy army elsewhere.
