古文：疑以叩实，察而后动。
现代文：一旦发现可疑情况就要调查落实，在调查清楚之后才能采取行动。
English: Once a suspicious situation is found, it must be investigated and implemented, and action can only be taken after the investigation is clear.

古文：复者，阴之媒也。
现代文：反复侦察，是发现阴谋的重要途径。
English: Repeated reconnaissance is an important way to discover conspiracies.
