古文：待天以困之，用人以诱之，往蹇来返。
现代文：在战场上，等到自然条件对敌人不利时再出击，用人为的假象去诱惑敌人。主动进攻有危险，诱敌来攻则有利。
English: On the battlefield, wait until the natural conditions are unfavorable to the enemy before attacking, and use artificial illusions to tempt the enemy. There is danger in attacking on one's own initiative, but it is advantageous to lure the enemy to attack.
