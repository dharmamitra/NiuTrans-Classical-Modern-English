古文：兵强者，攻其将；将智者，伐其情。
现代文：对军事力量强大的敌军，就攻击他们的将帅；对有智谋的将帅，要设法动摇他们的斗志。
English: If the enemy is militarily strong, attack their generals; if they are clever, try to shake their morale.

古文：将弱兵颓，其势自萎。
现代文：将帅斗志衰弱、部队士气消沉，战斗力自然萎缩。
English: The fighting spirit of the generals is weak, the morale of the troops is depressed, and the combat effectiveness naturally shrinks.

古文：利用御寇，顺相保也。
现代文：就像《周易渐》卦中所说的，利用敌人的弱点打击敌人，就能顺利地保护自己。
English: As it is said in the "Zhou Yi Qian" story, if you use the enemy's weakness to attack the enemy, you can successfully protect yourself.
