古文：疑中之疑。
现代文：在敌人给我方布置的疑阵中再布疑阵。
English: In the ambiguity that the enemy has placed on our side, we will once again deploy ambiguity.

古文：比之自内，不自失也。
现代文：顺势利用敌人派来的间谍做内应，为己所用，而自己则不会受到任何损害，这就是《周易比》中反间计的内容。
English: Taking advantage of the situation, using spies sent by the enemy to do internal correspondence, for their own use, while they themselves will not suffer any damage, this is the content of the countermeasures in "Zhou Yi Bi."
