古文：诳也，非诳也，实其所诳也。
现代文：运用假象欺骗对方，但不是弄假到底，而是让对方把受骗的假象当成真象。
English: Use deception to deceive the other party, but do not fake it to the end, but let the other party take the deceived deception as the truth.

古文：少阴、太阴、太阳。
现代文：用大大小小的假象去掩护真象。
English: To cover up the truth with large and small illusions.
