古文：微隙在所必乘，微利在所必得。
现代文：在大部队调遣、调动的过程中，敌人微小的漏洞必须利用，微小的利益，也必须获得。
English: In the process of dispatching and mobilizing large forces, the enemy must take advantage of small loopholes, and small benefits must also be obtained.

古文：少阴，少阳。
现代文：变敌人小的疏忽，为我方小的胜利。
English: It is a small victory for us to turn the enemy's negligence into a small victory.
