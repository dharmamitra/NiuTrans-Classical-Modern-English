古文：道生之，德畜之，物形之，势成之。
现代文：道生成万事万物，德养育万事万物。
English: Tao produces all things, and morality nurtures all things.

古文：是以万物莫不尊道而贵德。
现代文：万物呈现出各种各样的形态，环境使万物成长起来。故此，万事万物莫不尊崇道而重视德。
English: All things take on a variety of forms, and the environment causes all things to grow. Therefore, there is nothing that does not respect the Tao and value virtue.

古文：道之尊，德之贵，夫莫之命而常自然。
现代文：道之所以被尊崇，德所以被重视，就在于它不加干涉而顺其自然。
English: The reason why Tao is revered and virtue is highly valued is that it follows its own course without interference.

古文：故道生之，德畜之；长之育之，亭之毒之养之覆之。
现代文：因而，道生长万物，德养育万物，使万物生长发展，成熟结果，使其受到抚养、保护。
English: Therefore, Tao grows all things, virtue nurtures all things, causes all things to grow and develop, matures the results, and nurtures and protects them.

古文：生而不有，为而不恃，长而不宰。
现代文：它产生了万物却不据为已有，养育了万物却不自恃其功，导引了万物而不做万物的主宰。
English: It gave birth to all things without claiming to be the origin of all things, nurtured all things without taking credit for it, and guided all things without being the master of all things.

古文：是谓玄德。
现代文：就称作自然无为最高深的德性。
English: It is called natural helplessness, the deepest virtue.
