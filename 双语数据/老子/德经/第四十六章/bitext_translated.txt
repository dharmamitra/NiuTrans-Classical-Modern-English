古文：天下有道，却走马以粪。
现代文：治理天下合乎 道 ，就可以做到太平安定，把战马用来耕田种地。
English: If the governance of the world conforms to the Way, then peace and stability can be achieved, and war horses can be used to plow fields and cultivate land.

古文：天下无道，戎马生於郊。
现代文：治理天下不合乎 道 ，连怀胎的母马也要送上战场，在战场的郊外生下马驹子。
English: The governance of the world is not in accordance with the Tao, even pregnant mares are sent to the battlefield, and foals are born on the outskirts of the battlefield.

古文：祸莫大于不知足；咎莫大于欲得。
现代文：最大的祸害莫过于不满足，最大的过失莫过于贪得无厌。
English: The greatest of evils is dissatisfaction, the greatest of faults is insatiable greed.

古文：故知足之足，常足矣。
现代文：所以，知道欲望有度，不贪得无厌，才能保持恒久的满足。
English: Therefore, knowing that one's desires are moderate and one is not insatiably greedy is the only way to maintain lasting satisfaction.
