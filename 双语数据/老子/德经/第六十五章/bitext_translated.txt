古文：古之善为道者，非以明民，将以愚之。
现代文：古代善于用道治国的人，不会教导人民知晓智巧伪诈，而是教导人民质朴敦厚。
English: In ancient times, those who were good at governing the country with the Tao did not teach the people to be clever and cunning, but taught the people to be simple and dignified.

古文：民之难治，以其智多。
现代文：人民之所以难于统治，是因为他们使用太多的智巧心机。
English: The people are difficult to govern because they use too many ingenious devices.

古文：故以智治国，国之贼；不以智治国，国之福。
现代文：所以用智巧心机治理国家，就必然会危害国家，成为国家的灾祸；不用智巧心机治理国家，才是国家的幸福。
English: Therefore, governing the country with wisdom and skill will inevitably harm the country and become a national disaster; governing the country without wisdom and skill is the country's happiness.

古文：知此两者亦稽式。常知稽式，是谓 玄德 。 玄德 深矣，远矣，与物反矣，然后乃至大顺。
现代文：了解这两种治国方式的不同，而有所舍取就是一种法则，经常了解这个法则，就叫做 玄德 。玄德又深又远，和具体的事物复归到真朴，然后才能极大地顺乎于自然。
English: To understand the difference between these two ways of governing the country, and to give up something is a law, and to understand this law often is called "hypocrisy." Hypocrisy is deep and far, and concrete things are returned to simplicity, and then they can greatly follow nature.
