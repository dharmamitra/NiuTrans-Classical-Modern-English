古文：不出户，知天下；不窥牖，见天道。
现代文：了解大道的人不出门户，就能够推知天下的事理；不看一眼窗外，就可以了解大自然运行的规律。
English: People who know the thoroughfare can deduce the world's affairs without going to the portal; without looking out of the window, they can understand the laws of nature's operation.

古文：其出弥远，其知弥少。
现代文：向外奔逐得越远的人，所知道的道理就越少。
English: The farther a man is driven out, the less he knows.

古文：是以圣人不行而知，不见而明，不为而成。
现代文：所以，有 道 的圣人不出行却能够推知事理，不用窥探就能明了 天道 ，不去妄加施为就能够有所成就。
English: Therefore, a saint who has the way can lead the government without going out, can understand the heavenly way without prying, and can achieve something without doing too much.
