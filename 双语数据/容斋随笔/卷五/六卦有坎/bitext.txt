古文：《易》《乾》、《坤》二卦之下，继之以《屯》、《蒙》、《需》、《讼》、《师》、《比》。
现代文：《易经》中《乾》、《坤》二卦之下，接着有《屯》卦、《蒙》卦、《需》卦、《讼》卦、《师》卦、《比》卦。
English: In the I Ching, "Ken" and "Kun" are under the two gossips, followed by "Tun" gossip, "Mong" gossip, "Need" gossip, "Dispute" gossip, "Division" gossip, "Comparison" gossip.

古文：六者皆有坎，圣人防患备险之意深矣！
现代文：这六卦之中都有《坎卦》，圣人防患备险的意味可谓很深了！
English: Among these six sagas, there is "Kangwa," and the meaning of the sage's prevention and preparation of danger is very deep!
