古文：《乾》卦： 其惟圣人乎？ 
现代文：《乾》卦的卦词说； 难道只有圣人吗？ 
English: "Dry" is a word from the classical Chinese poem, "The Tale of the Goat"; is it only the sages who are involved?

古文：魏王肃本作 愚人 ，后结句始作 圣人 ，见陆德明《释文》。
现代文：曹魏王肃的本子作 愚人 ，最后的结句才作 圣人 ，这见于陆德明的《经典释文》一书。
English: Cao Wei Wang Su's book is called Fool, and the last sentence is called Saint, which is found in Lu Deming's "Classic Interpretation" book.
