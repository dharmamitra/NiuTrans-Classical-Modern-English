古文：刚毅者，必不能令色。木讷者，必不为巧言。
现代文：刚毅的人，必定不能和颜悦色，不善言辩的人，必定不说讨人喜欢的话。
English: Those who are strong-willed will not be able to be agreeable, and those who are not good at arguing will not be able to say pleasant things.

古文：此近仁鲜仁之辨也。
现代文：这就是分辫近仁少仁的要领。
English: This is the key to distinguishing between the near-benevolent and the less-benevolent.
