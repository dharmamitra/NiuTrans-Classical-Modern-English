古文：唐人呼县令为明府，丞为赞府，尉为少府。
现代文：唐人称呼县令为明府，称县丞为赞府，称县尉为少府。
English: The Tang people called the county magistrate Mingfu, the county magistrate Zhanfu, and the county magistrate Shaofu.

古文：《李太白集》有《饯阳曲王赞公贾少公石艾尹少公序》。
现代文：《李太白集》中有《饯阳曲王赞公贾少公石艾尹少公序》一文。
English: In the "Li Taibai Collection," there is an article entitled "Shuyang Qigong King Zangong Jia Shao Gongshi Ai Yin Shao Gong Xiu."

古文：盖阳曲丞、尉，石艾尉也。 赞公 ， 少公 之语益奇。
现代文：这篇文章是饯别阳曲县丞和县尉，以及石艾县尉时写的。文中变 赞府 为 赞公 ，变 少府 为 少公 ，这种称呼就更是奇特了。
English: This article was written to bid farewell to Zou Yang, Qu Xian Zheng, and Shiai Xian Zheng. In the article, Zangfu was changed to Zangong, and Shaofu was changed to Shaoong, which made the name even more peculiar.
