古文：翰苑故事，今废弃无余。
现代文：翰林院的传统制度，现今废弃得没有存余了。
English: Hanlin Academy's traditional system is now so obsolete that there is nothing left.

古文：唯学士入朝，犹有朱衣院吏双引至朝堂而止，及景灵宫行香，则引至立班处。
现代文：只有学士进朝时，还有穿红衣的院吏双双引路到达朝堂停下来；到景灵宫举行烧香仪式，就引路到应该站立的地方。
English: When entering the Imperial Palace, only the scholars and the red-robed courtiers stop to guide the way to the Imperial Palace; when the incense-burning ceremony is held in the Jingling Palace, they guide the way to the place where they should stand.

古文：公文至三省不用申状，但尺纸直书其事，右语云： 谘报尚书省伏候裁旨，月日押 ，谓之谘报。
现代文：公文送到三省不用专门上行的文书，只用一尺见方的纸直接写上要申报的事，前面写上： 公文上报尚书省，恭敬听侯裁定旨意，某月某日押字 ，这叫做谘报。
English: The official document sent to the three provinces does not need a special document, just use a square foot of paper to write directly on the matter to be declared, and write on the front: The official document is reported to the provincial government, respectfully listen to the will of the ruling, and stamp it on a certain day of a certain month, which is called the advisory report.

古文：此两事仅存。
现代文：只保存了这两件事。
English: Only those two things were saved.
