古文：朋友之义甚重。天下之达道五：君臣、父子、兄弟、夫妇而至朋友之交。
现代文：朋友的情义很重要，天下人共同遵循的道有五类：君臣之道，父子之道，兄弟之道，夫妇之道，以及朋友之道。
English: The feelings of friends are very important. There are five types of paths that people in the world follow together: the path of the ruler and the subject, the path of the father and the son, the path of the brother, the path of the husband and the wife, and the path of the friend.

古文：故天子至于庶人，未有不须友以成者。 天下俗薄，而朋友道绝 见于《诗》： 不信乎朋友，弗获乎上。 见于《中庸》、《孟子》； 朋友信之 ，孔子之志也； 车马衣裘，与朋友共， 子路之志也； 与朋友交而信 ，曾子之志也。
现代文：因此从天子到百姓，没有不需要朋友而成事的。 天下的习俗情义淡薄，因而朋友之道断绝 ，这话见于《诗经》； 不取信于朋友，就不能获得主上的恩遇 ，这话见于《中庸》、《孟子》； 朋友信任自己 ，这是孔子的志趣； 车驾、马匹、衣裳、皮裘，跟朋友共享 ，这是子路的志趣； 跟朋友交往而且守信 ，这是曾子的志趣。
English: Therefore, from the Son of Heaven to the people, there is nothing that can be done without friends. The customs of the world are weak, so the way of friends is cut off, this is found in the "Song of Songs"; If you don't trust your friends, you can't get the grace of the Lord, this is found in the "Middle Way" and "Mencius"; Friends trust themselves, this is the hobby of Confucius; Cars, horses, clothes, fur coats, sharing with friends, this is the hobby of the path; Interacting with friends and keeping faith, this is the hobby of the great-grandson.

古文：《周礼》六行，五曰任，谓信于友也。
现代文：《周礼》中有六种良好的品行，第五种就是信任，指的是对朋友的信任。
English: "Zhou Li" has six kinds of good conduct, and the fifth is trust, which refers to trust in friends.

English: Since the Han and Tang Dynasties, there have also been Paradigm and Zhang Xuan, Chen Chong, Lei Yi, Yuan Xuan, Bai Juyi, Liu Yuxi, Liu Zongyuan. These people have always been good friends, and they do not change their minds because of the nobility of life and death.
