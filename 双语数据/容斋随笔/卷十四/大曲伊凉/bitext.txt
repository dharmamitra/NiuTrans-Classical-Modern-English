古文：今乐府所传大曲，皆出于唐，而以州名者五，伊、凉、熙、石、渭也。
现代文：如今乐府诗所流传的大曲，都出自唐代，而以州名为名的有五种，就是伊州、凉州、熙州、石州、渭州。
English: The great songs that are now circulating in the Lefu Poetry Institute are all from the Tang Dynasty, and there are five that are named after states, namely, Izhou, Liangzhou, Xizhou, Shizhou, and Zhangzhou.

古文：凉州今转为梁州，唐人已多误用，其实从西凉府来也。
现代文：凉州现在变成了梁州，唐代已有许多人搞错了，其实是从西凉府来的。
English: Liangzhou has now become Liangzhou. During the Tang Dynasty, many people made the mistake of thinking that Liangzhou came from West Liangfu.

古文：凡此诸曲，唯伊、凉最著，唐诗词称之极多，聊纪十数联，以资谈助。
现代文：在这些大曲中，只有伊州，凉州最突出，唐代诗词很多都提到它们。姑且记下十几联，以供谈话的资料。
English: Among these great songs, only Izhou and Liangzhou are the most prominent, and many Tang Dynasty poems mention them. Let me take down a dozen couplets to provide information for the conversation.
