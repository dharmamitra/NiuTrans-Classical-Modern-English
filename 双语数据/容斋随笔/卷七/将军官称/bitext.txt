古文：《前汉书•百官表》： 将军皆周末官，秦因之。 
现代文：《汉书•百官表》中载： 将军都是周代末年的官，秦代沿用了这个称号。 
English: "Hanshu•Hundred Official List" contains: Generals are officials at the end of the Zhou Dynasty, and the Qin Dynasty continued to use this title.

古文：予按《国语》： 郑文公以詹伯为将军。 又： 吴夫差十旌一将军。 
现代文：据查，《国语》里有载： 郑文公以詹伯为将军 ，又载： 吴王夫差十旌一将军 。
English: According to the investigation, the "National Language" contains: Zheng Wengong used Zhanbo as a general, and also contains: Wu Wangfu is only a tenth of a general.

古文：《左传》： 岂将军食之而有不足。 
现代文：《左传》里记有 岂将军食之而有不足。 
English: In "Zo Zhuan" it is recorded that there was a shortage of cockroaches for the general to eat.

古文：《檀弓》： 卫将军。 
现代文：《檀弓》里有 卫将军 语。
English: "Danyu" has General Wei's speech.

古文：《文子》： 鲁使慎子为将军。 
现代文：《文子》里亦有 鲁国任用慎子为将军。 
English: There is also a story in "Fumiko" that the State of Lu appointed Shizuko as a general.

古文：然则其名人矣。
现代文：可见，将军的称号时间很久了。
English: Clearly, the title of general has been around for a long time.

古文：彭宠为奴所缚，呼其妻曰： 趣为诸将军办装。 
现代文：东汉时，彭宠为奴隶缚捆，他急忙喊叫他的妻子说： 快去为各位将军置办行装。 
English: During the Eastern Han Dynasty, when Peng Jia was tied up as a slave, he hurriedly called out to his wife, saying, "Quickly go and buy uniforms for the generals."

古文：《东汉书》注云： 呼奴为将军，欲其赦已也。 
现代文：《后汉书》中在这一句下作注说： 称呼奴隶为将军，是为了要他们释放自己。 
English: In the "Hou Han Shu" there is a note under this sentence: "To call a slave a general is to ask them to free themselves."

古文：今吴人语犹谓小苍头为将军，盖本诸此。
现代文：现在吴人仍称名叫小苍头的奴隶为将军，其根据也在于此。
English: This is also the reason why the people of Wu still call a slave named Xiao Cang Tou a general.
