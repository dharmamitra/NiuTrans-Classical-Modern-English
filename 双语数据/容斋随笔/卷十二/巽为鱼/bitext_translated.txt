古文：《易》卦所言鱼，皆指《巽》也。
现代文：《周易》卦中所说的鱼，都是指的《巽卦》。
English: The fish mentioned in "Zhou Yi" and "Gua" all refer to "Gua."

古文：《姤卦》《巽》下《乾》上，故九二有鱼，九四无鱼。《井》内卦为《巽》，故二有射鲋之象。
现代文：《姤卦》的卦体是《巽卦》在下、《乾卦》在上，因此它的九二爻的爻辞提到鱼，而九四艾爻没有鱼《井卦》的下位卦为《巽卦》，因此第二爻有射猎纷鱼的卦象。
English: The gaga body of "Ganga" is "Ganga" at the bottom and "Kengaga" at the top, so its ninety-two syllables of syllables mention fish, while there is no fish in the ninety-four syllables. The lower syllable of "Jingga" is "Ganga," so the second syllable has a gaga elephant that shoots fish.

古文：《中孚》外卦为《巽》，故曰 豚鱼吉 。
现代文：《中孚卦》的上位卦是《巽卦》，因此说 豚鱼吉 。
English: "Zhongfu Gua" is ranked higher than "Guan Gua," so it is called "Pork Fish Roast."

古文：《剥卦》五阴而一阳。
现代文：《剥封》有五个阴爻、一个阳爻。
English: "Stripping" has five female characters and one impotent character.

古文：方一阴自下生，变《乾》为《姤》，其下三爻，乃《巽》体也。
现代文：当一个阴爻刚从下面生出时，就从《乾卦》交成了《姤卦》，《姤卦》下位卦的三爻，就构成了《巽卦》的卦体。
English: When a yin and yang just emerged from below, it transformed from "Kenga" to "Yangga," and the three yang of the lower yang of "Yangga" formed the body of "Yangga."

古文：二阴生而为《遁》，则六二、九三、九四乃《巽》体。
现代文：两个阴爻生出，就成了《遁卦》，那么它的六二、九三、九四就构成《巽卦》的卦体。
English: When two yin and yang doves are born, it becomes "The Tale of the Goat," and then its sixty-two, ninety-three, and ninety-four doves constitute the body of "The Tale of the Goat."

古文：三阴生而为《否》，则六三、九四、九五乃《巽》体。四阴生而为《观》，则上三爻乃《巽》体。
现代文：三个阴爻生出，就成了《否卦》，那么六三、九四、九五就构成《巽卦》的卦体，四个阴爻生出，那么上位卦的三爻就是《巽卦》卦体。
English: If three yin gongs are produced, it will become "Negative," then the 63rd, 94th, and 95th gongs will constitute the body of "Gong," and if four yin gongs are produced, then the three gongs of the superior gong will be the body of "Gong."

古文：至五阴为《剥》，则《巽》始亡。
现代文：到五个阴爻在下就成了《剥卦》，那么《巽卦》的卦体就没有了。
English: When the five yin and yang are at the bottom, it becomes "stripping," and then the body of "guanxi" is no more.

古文：故六五之爻辞曰： 贯鱼 ，盖指下四爻皆从《巽》来，如鱼骈头而贯也。
现代文：因此《剥卦》的六五爻的爻辞说到 贯鱼 ，指的是下面的四爻都是从《巽卦》来的，就象鱼头尾相接依次穿过。
English: Therefore, the words of the sixty-five syllables of the "Peeling Gua" referred to the "crossing fish," which meant that the following four syllables were all from the "Peeling Gua," just like the fish passing through the head and tail in succession.

古文：或曰： 《说卦》不言 《巽》为鱼 ，今何以知之？ 
现代文：有人说： 《说卦传》没有说 《巽卦》的象义是鱼 ，现在怎么会知道它是鱼呢？ 
English: Some people said: "The Analects of Confucius" did not say that the symbolic meaning of "The Analects of Confucius" is fish, so how can we know that it is fish?

古文：曰： 以类而知之，《说卦》所不该者多矣。
现代文：我的回答是： 根据类推就可以知道，《说卦传》所没有全面提到的多了。
English: My answer is: According to the analogy, it can be known that there are many things that are not fully mentioned in "The Tale of the Goat."

古文：如 长子 、 长女 、 中女 、 少女 见于《震》、《巽》、《离》、《兑》中，而《坎》、《艮》之下，不言 为中男 、 为少男 之类，他可推也。 
现代文：例如 长子 、 长女 、 中女 、 少女 在《震卦》、《巽卦》、《离卦》、《兑卦》中出现，而《坎卦》、《艮卦》等等，却不提 为中男 、 为少男 之类的话，这是因为其他可以类推的缘故。 
English: For example, the eldest son, eldest daughter, middle daughter, and young girl appear in "Zhen Gua," "Jing Gua," "Leaving Gua," and "Reciprocating Gua," but "Kang Gua," "Jing Gua," and so on, do not mention such words as "middle man" and "young man." This is because others can be inferred by analogy.
