古文：四年春正月，公狩于郎。
现代文：四年春季正月，桓公在郎地狩猎。
English: In the spring of the fourth year, in the first month, Prince Lu Huan went hunting in the countryside.

古文：夏，天王使宰渠伯纠来聘。
现代文：夏季，周天子派遣宰官渠伯纠来鲁国聘问。
English: In the summer, Zhou Tianzi sent the vice-governor Qu Bo to inquire about the situation in Lu.

古文：四年春正月，公狩于郎。
现代文：四年春季，正月，鲁桓公在郎地打猎。
English: In the spring of the fourth year, in the first month, Prince Lu Huan went hunting in the countryside.

古文：书时，礼也。夏，周宰渠伯纠来聘。
现代文：《春秋》记载这件事，是由于这正是狩猎之时，合于礼。夏季，周朝的宰官渠伯纠来鲁国聘问。
English: "Spring and Autumn" recorded this event because it was the time of hunting, which was in accordance with the rite. In the summer, the Zhou Dynasty's vice-governor, Qu Bo, came to Lu for an interview.

古文：父在，故名。
现代文：由于他的父亲还活着，所以《春秋》写出他的名字。
English: Since his father is still alive, "Spring and Autumn" writes his name.

古文：秋，秦师侵芮，败焉，小之也。
现代文：秋季，秦国的军队袭击芮国，秦军战败，由于小看了敌人的缘故。
English: In autumn, the army of Qin attacked the State of Yao. The Qin army was defeated because it underestimated the enemy.

古文：冬，王师、秦师围魏，执芮伯以归。
现代文：冬季，周桓王的军队和秦国军队联合包围芮国，俘虏了芮伯回来。
English: In winter, the army of King Zhou and the army of Qin jointly besieged Yunnan and captured Zibo.
