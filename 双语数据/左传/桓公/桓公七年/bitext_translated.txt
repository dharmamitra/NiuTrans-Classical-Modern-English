古文：七年春二月己亥，焚咸丘。
现代文：七年，春天，二月，己亥这一天，咸丘被焚烧。
English: In the spring of the seventh year, in February, on the day of Ji Hai, Hamgyu was burned.

古文：夏，穀伯绥来朝。
现代文：夏天，毅国国君绥来鲁国朝见。
English: In the summer, Yi Guo Guojun came to see the imperial court of Lu.

古文：邓侯吾离来朝。
现代文：邓国国君来鲁国朝见。
English: Deng Guo Guojun came to see the Imperial Court of Lu.

古文：七年春，穀伯、邓侯来朝。
现代文：七年春季，穀伯绥、邓侯吾离来鲁国朝见。
English: In the spring of the seventh year, Gu Boqin and Deng Hougu left Lu to see the imperial court.

古文：名，贱之也。
现代文：《春秋》记载他们的名字，是由于轻视他们。
English: "Spring and Autumn" recorded their names because they despised them.

古文：夏，盟、向求成于郑，既而背之。
现代文：夏季，盟邑、向邑向郑国求和，不久又背叛郑国。
English: In the summer, Mengyur and Xiangyur sued for peace with Zheng and soon betrayed Zheng.

古文：秋，郑人、齐人、卫人伐盟、向。
现代文：秋季，郑军、齐军、卫军攻打盟邑、向邑。
English: In autumn, Zheng's army, Qi's army, and Wei's army attacked Lianyungang and Xiangyungang.

古文：王迁盟、向之民于郏。
现代文：周桓王把盟邑、向邑的百姓迁到郏地。
English: King Zhou Kuan moved the people of Mengyur and Xiangyur to Shiji.

古文：冬，曲沃伯诱晋小子侯，杀之。
现代文：冬季，曲沃伯诱骗晋国小子侯，把他杀死了。
English: In winter, Qu Wo Bo tricked Jin Guo Xiaozhong and killed him.
