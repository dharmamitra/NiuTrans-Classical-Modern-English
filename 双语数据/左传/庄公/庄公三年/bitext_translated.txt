古文：三年春，王正月，溺会齐师伐卫。
现代文：三年春季，周历正月，公子溺会合齐国军队攻打卫国。
English: In the spring of the third year, in the first month of the lunar calendar, Gongzidang gathered the Qi army to attack and defend the country.

古文：夏四月，葬宋庄公。
现代文：夏季四月，安葬宋庄公。
English: In the summer of April, Song Zhuang Gong was buried.

古文：五月，葬桓王。
现代文：五月，安葬周桓王。
English: In May, King Zhou Kuan was buried.

古文：秋，纪季以酅入于齐。
现代文：秋季，纪季把酅地割让给齐国。
English: In the autumn, Ji Ji ceded the Lijiang region to Qi.

古文：冬，公次于滑。
现代文：冬季，鲁庄公带领护卫军屯驻在滑地多夜。
English: In winter, the Duke of Luzhuang led the escort troops to stay in the landslide area for many nights.

古文：三年春，溺会齐师伐卫，疾之也。
现代文：三年春季，公子溺会合齐国军队攻打卫国，《春秋》单称他的名字溺，不称公子，是表示对他的贬斥。
English: In the spring of the third year, Gongzi Dongrui joined the Qi army to attack and defend the country. "Spring and Autumn" called his name Dongrui, not Gongzi, to denigrate him.

古文：夏五月，葬桓王，缓也。
现代文：夏季五月，安葬周桓王。
English: In the summer of May, King Zhou Kuan was buried.

古文：秋，纪季以酅入于齐，纪于是乎始判。
现代文：这在丧礼的时间上太迟缓了。秋季，纪季把酅地割让给齐国，纪国从这时候开始分裂。
English: This was too late for a funeral. In the autumn, Ji Ji ceded the land to the State of Qi, and from this time the State of Qi began to split.

古文：冬，公次于滑，将会郑伯谋纪故也。
现代文：冬季，鲁庄公带领护卫军屯驻在滑地多夜，打算会见郑伯，策划纪国的事务。
English: In winter, Lu Zhuang Gong led the escort troops to stay in the landslide for many nights, intending to meet with Zheng Bo and plan the affairs of Ji Guo.

古文：郑伯辞以难。
现代文：郑伯用国内不安定为理由加以推脱。
English: Zheng Bo used domestic instability as a reason to escape.

古文：凡师一宿为舍，再宿为信，过信为次。
现代文：凡是军队在外，住一夜叫做舍，两夜叫做信，两夜以上叫做次。
English: When the army is away, one night's stay is called lodging, two nights is called lodging, and more than two nights is called secondment.
