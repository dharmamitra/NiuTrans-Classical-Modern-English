古文：鲁襄公使叔孙穆子来聘，范宣子问焉，曰： 人有言曰 死而不朽 ，何谓也？ 
现代文：鲁襄公派叔孙穆子出使晋国，范宣子问他说： 古人有句话说 死而不朽 ，这是什么意思呢？ 
English: Luliangong sent his uncle and grandson, Mu Zi, as an envoy to Jin. Fan Xuan Zi asked him, "The ancients had a saying, 'Death and immortality.' What does this mean?"

古文：穆子未对。
现代文：穆子没有回答。
English: Muzi did not answer.

古文：宣子曰： 昔匄之祖，自虞以上为陶唐氏，在夏为御龙氏，在商为豕韦氏，在周为唐、杜氏。
现代文：范宣子说： 从前我的祖先，在虞舜以前是陶唐氏，在夏朝时是御龙氏，在商朝是豕韦氏，在周朝是唐、杜氏。
English: Fan Xuan Zi said, "Once upon a time, my ancestors were the Tao and Tang dynasties before the Yuezhi dynasty, the Goryong dynasty during the Xia dynasty, the Wei dynasty during the Shang dynasty, and the Tang and Du dynasties during the Zhou dynasty.

古文：周卑，晋继之，为范氏，其此之谓也？ 
现代文：周王室衰微以后，晋国继为盟主，叫做范氏。所谓 死而不朽 ，恐怕就是指此而言吧？ 
English: After the decline of the Zhou royal family, Jin Province became the leader, called the Fan clan. The so-called death and immortality, I'm afraid, refers to this, right?

古文：对曰： 以豹所闻，此之谓世禄，非不朽也。
现代文：穆子回答说： 据我所听到的，这叫做世代享有禄位，并非不朽。
English: Muzi replied, "From what I have heard, this is called generationally enjoying a position, not immortality.

古文：鲁先大夫臧文仲，其身殁矣，其言立于后世，此之谓死而不朽。 
现代文：鲁国已故大夫臧文仲，他身虽死，言论还流传在后世，这才叫做 死而不朽。  
English: According to the late Dr. Zou Wenzhong of the State of Lu, even though he is dead, his words will still be passed down to future generations. This is called death and immortality.
