古文：献公问于卜偃曰： 攻虢何月也？ 
现代文：晋献公问掌卜大夫郭偃道： 攻打虢国应该在哪个月份？ 
English: Jin Xian Gong asked the palm doctor Guo Xingdao: In which month should the attack on Xingguo begin?

古文：对曰： 童谣有之曰： 丙之晨，龙尾伏辰，均服振振，取虢之旗。
现代文：回答说： 童谣中有这样的话： 丙子日的早晨，日月交会在龙尾星辰，兵士的军服成风齐整，去夺取虢国的旗旌。
English: He replied, "There is a nursery rhyme that says, 'On the morning of the day of the son of the dragon, the sun and the moon will meet at the tail of the dragon and the stars, and the soldiers' uniforms will be in order, and they will go to seize the banner of the country of the dragon.'

古文：鹑之贲贲，天策焞焞，火中成军，虢公其奔！ 
现代文：鹑火星贲贲，天策星焞焞，早晨集合大军去进攻，虢公将要仓皇出奔。 
English: In the morning, the great army will gather to attack, and the prince will run away in a panic.

古文：火中而旦，其九月十月之交乎？ 
现代文：鹑火星在早晨出现在南方，该是九月底到十月初之间吧？ 
English: Mars appears in the south in the morning. Is it between the end of September and the beginning of October?
