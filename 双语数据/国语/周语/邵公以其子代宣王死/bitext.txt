古文：彘之乱，宣王在邵公之宫。国人围之。
现代文：在周厉王逃奔彘的动乱中，他的儿子宣王在召公家避难，国人包围了召公的住处。
English: In the turmoil of Zhou Li Wang's flight, his son, Xuan Wang, was calling on the court nobles to take refuge, and the people of the country surrounded the residence of the caller.

古文：邵公曰： 昔吾骤谏王，王不从，是以及此难。
现代文：召公说： 过去我多次劝谏厉王，厉王都不听，所以罹受如此的祸难。
English: Zhao Gong said, "In the past, I have repeatedly advised King Li, but King Li did not listen, so I have suffered such a misfortune.

古文：今杀王子，王其以我为怼而怒乎！
现代文：现在假如太子被杀，厉王将以为我是因为怨愤而在发怒。
English: Now, if the crown prince is killed, King Li will think that I am angry out of resentment.

古文：夫事君者险而不怼，怨而不怒，况事王乎？ 
现代文：事奉国君遇到危险不埋怨，有怨气不发怒，何况事奉天子呢？ 
English: If you serve the sovereign, you will not complain in the face of danger. If you have a grudge, you will not get angry. How much more so if you serve the Son of Heaven?

古文：乃以其子代宣王。
现代文：于是就用自己的儿子顶替宣王去死。
English: So he used his own son to die in place of King Xuan.

古文：宣王长而立之。
现代文：宣王长大后召公扶持他继承了王位。
English: When King Xuan grew up, he called for public support and succeeded to the throne.
