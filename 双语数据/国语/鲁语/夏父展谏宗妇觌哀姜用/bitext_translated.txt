古文：哀姜至，公使大夫、宗妇觌用币。宗人夏父展曰： 非故也。 
现代文：哀姜来到鲁国，庄公让同宗大夫的妻子们带上玉、帛之类的礼物去拜见她。宗人夏父展说： 这不是先王的规矩。 
English: When Aizhang came to the State of Lu, Zhuang Gong asked the wives of the doctor of the same family, Zhuang, to bring gifts such as jade and enamel to visit her. Zongren Xia Fuchen said: This is not the rule of the former king.

古文：公曰： 君作故。 
现代文：庄公说： 国君可以创造规矩。 
English: Zhuang Gong said: The sovereign can create rules.

古文：对曰： 君作而顺则故之，逆则亦书其逆也。
现代文：夏父展回答说： 国君的创制合乎礼就能成为规矩，违反礼也将在史书上记载他违礼。
English: Xia Fuzhen replied: The creation of the sovereign can become a rule if it is in accordance with etiquette, and his violation of etiquette will also be recorded in the history books.

古文：臣从有司，惧逆之书于后也，故不敢不告。
现代文：我服从自己的职守，生怕这种违反礼的事情被记载下来传于后世，所以不敢不告诉你。
English: I obey my duty, for fear that this breach of etiquette will be recorded and passed on to future generations, so I dare not not tell you.

古文：夫妇贽不过枣、栗，以告虔也。
现代文：女人进见的礼物不过是枣、栗之类，用以表示诚敬。
English: A woman's idea of a gift is just dates, chestnuts and the like, used to show respect.

古文：男则玉、帛、禽、鸟，以章物也。
现代文：男人进见的礼物则有珠玉、丝帛、禽鸟之类，用以表明尊卑不同的身份和等级。
English: Men's gifts include precious stones, silk cocoons, birds and the like, which are used to show the different status and class of superiority and inferiority.

古文：今妇贽币，是男女无别也。
现代文：现在女人拿着玉、帛一类的礼物，这样男女就没有差别了。
English: Now, women hold gifts such as jade and enamel, so there is no difference between men and women.

古文：男女之别，国之大节也，不可无也。 
现代文：男女的区别，是国家的大礼节，不可以没有。 
English: The difference between men and women is a great courtesy of the state, which cannot be ignored.

古文：公弗听。
现代文：庄公没有听从。
English: Zhuang didn't listen.
