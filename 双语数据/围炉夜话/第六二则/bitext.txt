古文：读《论语》公子荆一章，富者可以为法；读《论语》齐景公一章，贫者可以自兴。
现代文：读《论语•子路篇》公子荆那章，可以让富有的人效法；读《论语•季氏篇》有关齐景公那一章，贫穷的人可以为之而奋发。
English: Reading the chapter of "The Analects of Confucius and the Sons of Confucius" on Gongzi Jing, the rich can follow the example; reading the chapter of "The Analects of Confucius and the Sons of Confucius" on Qi Jinggong, the poor can work hard for it.

古文：舍不得钱，不能为义士；舍不得命，不能为忠臣。
现代文：舍不得钱财，就不可能成为义士；舍不得性命，就不能成为忠臣。
English: If you don't care about money, you can't be a righteous person; if you don't care about your life, you can't be a loyal subject.
