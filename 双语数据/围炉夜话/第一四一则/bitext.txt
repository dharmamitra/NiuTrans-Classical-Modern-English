古文：成就人才，即是栽培子弟；暴殄天物，自应折磨儿孙。
现代文：培养有才能的人，就是培养自己的后辈有所成就。不知爱惜物力而任意浪费东西，自然使儿孙未来受苦受难。
English: To nurture talented people is to nurture the achievements of one's own offspring. If you do not know how to spare material resources and waste things arbitrarily, it will naturally make your children and grandchildren suffer in the future.
