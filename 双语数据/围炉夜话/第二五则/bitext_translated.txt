古文：大丈夫处事，论是非，不论祸福；士君子立言，贵平正，尤贵精详。
现代文：有志气的人在处理事情时，只考虑这件事是否做得正确，并不考虑这样做为自己带来的究竟是福是祸；读书人在写文章或是著书立说的时候，最重要的是立论要公平公正，若能更进一步去要求精要详尽，那就更可贵了。
English: When dealing with things, ambitious people only consider whether the matter is done correctly, and do not consider whether it is a blessing or a misfortune to do so. When writing an article or writing a book, the most important thing is to make a fair and just argument. If you can go further to require precision and detail, it will be more valuable.
