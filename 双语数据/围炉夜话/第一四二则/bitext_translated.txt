古文：和气迎人，平情应物。
现代文：以平和的态度去和人交往，以平等的心情去应对事物。
English: To interact with people with a peaceful attitude and to deal with things with an equal mind.

古文：抗心希古，藏器待时。
现代文：以古人的高尚心志自相期许，守住自己的才能以等待可用的时机。
English: With the high-mindedness of the ancients, they are self-expecting, holding on to their talents to wait for the available opportunity.
