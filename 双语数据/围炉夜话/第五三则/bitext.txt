古文：凡事谨守规模，必不大错；一生但足衣食，便称小康。
现代文：凡事只要谨慎地守着一定的规则与模式，总不致于出什么大的差错。一辈子只要衣食无忧，家境便可算是自给自足了。
English: As long as you carefully follow certain rules and patterns in everything, you will never make any big mistakes. As long as you have food and clothing for a lifetime, your family can be considered self-sufficient.
