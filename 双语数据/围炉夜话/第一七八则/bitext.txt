古文：待人宜宽，惟待子孙不可宽；行礼宜厚，惟行嫁娶不必厚。
现代文：对待他人应该宽厚，只有在对待子孙后代时不可太宽容。礼节要周到，惟有在办婚事时不必大肆铺张。
English: You should be kind to others, but you should not be too tolerant when it comes to your children and grandchildren. You should be considerate in your etiquette, but you should not be extravagant when it comes to marriage.
