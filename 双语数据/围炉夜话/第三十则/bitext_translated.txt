古文：志不可不高，志不高，则同流合污，无足有为矣；心不可太大，心太大，则舍近图远，难期有成矣。
现代文：一个人的志向不能不高远，如果志向不高，就容易为不良的环境所影响，不可能有什么大作为。一个人的野心不能够太大，如果野心太大，那么便会舍弃切近可行的事，而去追逐遥远不可达的目标，最终很难有所成就。
English: A person's ambition should not be too high. If the ambition is not high, it is easy to be affected by the bad environment, and it is impossible to do anything great. A person's ambition should not be too great. If the ambition is too great, then it will abandon what is feasible and pursue distant and unattainable goals. In the end, it is difficult to achieve something.
