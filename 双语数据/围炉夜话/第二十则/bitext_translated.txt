古文：心能辨事非，处事方能决断；人不忘廉耻，立身自不卑污。
现代文：心中能够辨别是非，在处理事情的时候，就能毫不犹豫地决定该怎么办；人要不忘记廉耻心，为人处世自然就不会做出任何卑鄙污秽的事。
English: If you can distinguish right and wrong in your heart, you can decide what to do without hesitation when dealing with things; if you do not forget your sense of shame, you will naturally not do any despicable and filthy things when dealing with people.
