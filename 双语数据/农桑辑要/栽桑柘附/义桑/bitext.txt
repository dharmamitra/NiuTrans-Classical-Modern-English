古文：《务本新书》：假有一村，两家相合，低筑围墙，四面各一百步，若户多地宽，更甚省力。
现代文：《务本新书》：假如在一个村子中，有两户人家互相合作，修筑矮围墙，四面各长一百步，如参加的户数多，地面宽广，还会更为省力。
English: "Ryuhon Shinsho": If in a village, two households cooperate with each other to build a low fence, each side of which is a hundred paces long, if the number of participating households is large and the ground is wide, it will be more labor-saving.

古文：一家该筑二百步。
现代文：一家应该筑二百步墙。
English: A family should build a 200-step wall.

古文：墙内空地，计一万步，每一步一桑，计一万株；一家计分五千株。
现代文：墙内空地总计有一万平方步。每平方步植桑一株，可栽桑一万株，每户可分得桑树五千株。
English: There are a total of 10,000 square meters of open space inside the wall. For each square meter of space, one mulberry tree can be planted, and 10,000 mulberry trees can be planted, and 5,000 mulberry trees can be distributed to each household.

古文：若一家孤另一转，筑墙二百步，墙内空地止二千五百步；依上一步一桑，止得二千五百株。
现代文：若是两家单独去干，修筑四面围墙共长二百步的桑园，园内的空地便只有二千五百平方步。依上法，每平方步栽桑一株，却只能栽桑二千五百株。
English: If two families go it alone and build a mulberry garden with four walls and a total length of two hundred paces, the open space in the garden will only be twenty-five hundred square paces. According to the law, one mulberry is planted per square step, but only twenty-five hundred mulberries can be planted.

古文：其功利不侔如此。
现代文：其中的利益，竟如此无法相比。
English: The benefits are beyond comparison.

古文：恐起争端，当于园心以篱介断。
现代文：如害怕发生纠纷，可以将桑园从中间用篱墙隔开。
English: If you are afraid of a dispute, you can separate the mulberry garden from the middle with a fence.

古文：比之独力筑墙，不止桑多一倍，亦递相藉力，容易句当。
现代文：这样做比起单独修围墙，不仅可以多栽一倍桑树，而且可以彼此换工，容易办事。
English: In this way, compared to repairing the fence alone, not only can twice as many mulberry trees be planted, but also the work can be exchanged with each other, which is easy to do.
