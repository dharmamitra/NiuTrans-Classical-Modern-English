古文：《齐民要术》：《春秋考异邮》曰： 蚕，阳物，大恶水，故蚕食而不饮。 
现代文：《齐民要术》：《春秋考异邮》说： 蚕性属阳，阳为火，火忌水，所以蚕只吃叶，不饮水。 
English: "Qi Min Yaoshu": "Spring and Autumn Examination of Foreign Mail" said: Silkworm sex is yang, yang is fire, fire is averse to water, so silkworms only eat leaves and do not drink water.

古文：《士农必用》：蚕之性；子在连则宜极寒；成蚁，则宜极暖；停眠起，宜温；大眠后，宜凉；临老，宜渐暖；入簇，则宜极暖。
现代文：《士农必用》：蚕的特性为：蚕卵在蚕种纸，要求低温；蚁蚕孵出时，要求非常暖和；停眠初起，要求温暖；大眠过后，要凉；临近老熟，要渐暖；入簇之后，要极暖。
English: "Essential for farmers": The characteristics of silkworms are as follows: the silkworm eggs require low temperature when the silkworm is growing paper; when the ant silkworm hatches, it requires very warm; at the beginning of dormancy, it requires warmth; after a long sleep, it needs to be cool; near maturity, it needs to be gradually warm; after entering the cluster, it needs to be extremely warm.
