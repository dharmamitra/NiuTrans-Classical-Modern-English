古文：《齐民要术》：凡荞麦，五月耕；经二十五日，草烂，得转；并种，耕三遍。
现代文：《齐民要术》：荞麦地，五月的时候耕一遍；经过二十五天，等草腐烂后，再耕一遍；播种时耕一遍，一共要耕三遍。
English: "Qimin Yaoshu": In the buckwheat field, it is necessary to plow it once in May; after twenty-five days, after the grass has rotted, it is necessary to plow it again; when sowing, it is necessary to plow it once, and it is necessary to plow it three times in total.

古文：立秋前后，皆十日内种之。
现代文：一律要在立秋前后的十天以内，进行播种。
English: All crops must be sown within ten days before and after the beginning of autumn.

古文：假如耕地三遍，即三重著子。
现代文：假如播种前是耕了三遍地，荞麦长成后，便会结三层子。
English: If the land is ploughed three times before sowing, after the buckwheat grows, it will produce three layers of seeds.

古文：下两重子黑，上一重子白，皆是白汁，满似如浓，即须收刈之。
现代文：当下面的两层子已变成黑颜色，只剩最上的一层子还是白颜色，而且所有的子都已灌满了如脓的白浆时，便应当即时收刈。
English: When the bottom two layers have turned black and only the top layer is still white, and all the seeds have been filled with maggot-like white slurry, they should be harvested immediately.

古文：但对梢苔铺之，其白者，日渐尽变为黑。
现代文：刈倒的荞麦，只须梢对梢搭摞在一起，平放在场中，白子便会一天天地变黑。
English: Cut down buckwheat, only need to tie the stalks to the stalks together, and lay it flat in the field, the white will turn black day by day.

古文：如此，乃为得所。
现代文：这样做才是好方法。
English: That's the way to do it.

古文：若待上头总黑半，已下黑子尽落矣。
现代文：假如等待最上一层的白子黑一半再收刈，下两层的黑子就会早已落光了。
English: If you wait for the top layer of white and black half to be harvested, the bottom two layers of black will have already been lost.
