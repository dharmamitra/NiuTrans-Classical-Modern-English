古文：《蚕经》：白光向食；青光厚饲，皮皱为饥；黄光，以渐住食。
现代文：《蚕经》：体色变白光的是开食期；体色变青光的是盛食期，皮肤有皱纹表示蚕饥饿；体色变黄光，应逐渐停止喂养。
English: "Silkworm Sutra": the body color turns white during the feeding period; the body color turns blue during the feeding period, and the wrinkles on the skin indicate that the silkworm is hungry; the body color turns yellow, and feeding should be gradually stopped.
