古文：《务本新书》：秋深，桑叶未黄，多广收拾，曝干，捣碎，于无烟火处收顿。
现代文：《务本新书》：秋末，桑叶还没有变黄的时候，应该想办法多收集一些，并且要即时晒干捣碎，贮放在没有烟火的地方。
English: "Ryuhon Shinsho": At the end of autumn, when the mulberry leaves have not yet turned yellow, you should find a way to collect more, and they should be dried and mashed immediately and stored in a place without fireworks.

古文：春蚕大眠后用。
现代文：准备来年春蚕大眠后使用。
English: Ready for use next spring after the silkworms have gone to sleep.

古文：《士农必用》：桑欲落时，捋叶。
现代文：《士农必用》：桑叶即将掉落的时候，捋取桑叶。
English: "Essential for farmers": When mulberry leaves are about to fall, pick up mulberry leaves.

古文：未欲落捋，伤来年桑眼；已落者，短津味，泥封收囤。
现代文：不到在桑叶还没有落的时候捋采，会损伤明年的宿芽；拾取已经落下的叶子，又枯燥无滋味。收后应泥封贮藏。
English: Do not harvest when the mulberry leaves have not yet fallen, it will damage next year's buds; picking up the fallen leaves is boring and tasteless. It should be sealed with mud after collection.

古文：至腊月内，捣磨成面。
现代文：到腊月间，捣碎磨成粉。
English: In the meantime, mash and grind into a powder.

古文：腊月内制者，能消蚕热病。
现代文：腊月间磨制成的粉，能消除蚕的热病。
English: The powder made from wax ground between the months can eliminate heat illness in silkworms.

古文：瓮器内，可多收。饲蚕余剩作牛料，牛甚美食。
现代文：瓮器容纳得下，便可多收，饲蚕有余剩，可做牛的饲料，牛非常爱吃
English: If you have enough reeds, you can collect more. If you have leftover silkworms, you can use them as fodder for cattle. Cows are very fond of cormorants
