古文：西川、唐、邓，多有栽种成就；怀州亦有旧日橙树。
现代文：橙，四川西部、河南唐河县、河南邓县各地，多有栽种，而且取得了成功。怀州也有过去栽种的老橙树存活着。
English: In western Sichuan, Tanghe County of Henan Province and Deng County of Henan Province, many orange trees have been planted and have achieved success. There are also old orange trees planted in the past in Huizhou.

古文：北地不见此种，若于附近地面访学栽植，甚得济用。
现代文：但北方却不见有栽种橙树的，假如能就近学习栽培技术，一定可以种树补贴家用。
English: However, there are no orange trees in the north. If you can learn cultivation techniques nearby, you can definitely plant trees to subsidize your household.

古文：柑，与橙同。
现代文：柑，和橙相同。
English: Citrus, same as orange.
