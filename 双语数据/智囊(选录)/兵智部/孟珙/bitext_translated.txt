古文：孟珙攻蔡。
现代文：原文
English: Original text from

古文：蔡人恃柴潭为固，外即汝河。
现代文：宋朝时孟珙攻打蔡州，当地人凭借柴潭险要的地形，据潭固守。
English: During the Song Dynasty, when Meng Xuan attacked Caizhou, the local people relied on the terrain of Chai Tan to defend themselves.

古文：潭高于河五六丈，城上金字号楼伏巨弩，相传下有龙，人不敢近。将士疑畏。
现代文：柴潭的外围就是汝河，潭底比汝河的河面要高出五六丈，城上有座金字匾额的城楼，城楼上设置有巨大的弓弩。柴潭水非常深，相传里面有龙潜藏，人们不敢轻易接近，孟珙的手下也对这个传说心生畏惧。
English: The periphery of Chai Tan is the Ye River. The bottom of the lake is five or six meters higher than the surface of the Ye River. There is a gold-lettered tower on the city, and there are huge bows on the tower. Chai Tan is very deep, and it is said that there is a dragon lurking inside. People dare not approach it easily, and Meng Ye's men are also afraid of this legend.

古文：珙召麾下饮酒，再行，谓曰： 此潭楼非天造地设，伏弩能及远，而不可射近，彼所恃，此水耳。
现代文：孟珙宴请各位将领，酒过三巡之后，孟珙说： 其实这座潭楼不是天险，楼台上的巨型大弓弩，只能远射，而不能近射，敌人所凭借的只是这潭水。
English: Meng Yao invited the generals to a banquet. After three rounds of drinks, Meng Yao said: Actually, this pool is not a natural hazard. The giant bow on the platform can only shoot far, but not close. The enemy only relies on this pool of water.

古文：决而注之，涸可立待。 
现代文：如果我们能够将潭水决开引入汝河之中，那么就可以等待潭水干涸。 
English: If we can break the swamp into the river, then we can wait for the swamp to dry up.

古文：遣人凿其两翼，潭果决，实以薪苇，遂济师，攻城克之。
现代文：于是派人从潭水的两侧凿开一条水道，将潭水引入汝河中，再在干涸的潭底铺上木柴、苇草，于是全军安然渡潭，攻打下城楼。
English: Therefore, they sent someone to dig a channel from both sides of the pool, and then introduced the pool water into the Yue River, and then spread firewood and weeds on the dry bottom of the pool, so the whole army crossed the pool safely and attacked the castle.
