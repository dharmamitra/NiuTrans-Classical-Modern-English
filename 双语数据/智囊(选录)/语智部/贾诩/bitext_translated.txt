古文：贾诩事操。
现代文：原文
English: Original text from

古文：时临淄侯植才名方盛，操尝欲废丕立植。
现代文：三国时贾诩为曹操属臣，这时临淄侯曹植才名极盛，曹操有意废太子曹丕而改立曹植。
English: At the time of the Three Kingdoms, Jia Jia was a vassal of Cao Cao. At that time, the name of Cao Zhi, the prince of Linyi, was at its peak. Cao Cao intentionally deposed Prince Cao Zhi and replaced him with Cao Zhi.

古文：一日屏左右问诩，诩默不对。操曰： 与卿言，不答，何也？ 
现代文：一天，曹操命左右退下，与贾诩商议改立太子的事，贾诩久不出声，曹操说： 我跟贤卿说话，贤卿怎么不做声呢？ 
English: One day, Cao Cao was ordered to step back and consult with Jia Ye on the matter of replacing the crown prince. Jia Ye did not speak for a long time. Cao Cao said, "I'm talking to the sage, why doesn't the sage speak?"

古文：对曰： 属有所思。 
现代文：贾诩说： 臣正在想一件事。 
English: Jia Wei said: The minister is thinking about something.

古文：操曰： 何思？ 
现代文：曹操又问： 贤卿想什么呢？ 
English: Cao Cao also asked: What does the sage think?

古文：诩曰： 思袁本初、刘景升父子。 
现代文：贾诩说： 我在想袁本初和刘景升两家父子的事。 
English: Jia Wei said: I am thinking about Yuan Benchu and Liu Jingsheng's father and son.

古文：操大笑，丕位遂定。
现代文：曹操听了哈哈大笑，从此曹丕太子的地位乃告确立。
English: Cao Cao laughed loudly when he heard this, and from then on Cao Pi's position as crown prince was firmly established.

古文：卫瓘 此座可惜 一语，不下于诩，晋武悟而不从，以致于败。
现代文：晋朝时卫瓘也有同样的故事，而且卫瓘的机智与含蓄不亚于贾诩，可惜晋武帝领悟后却不采纳，以致最后失败。
English: It was the same story with Emperor Wei of the Jin Dynasty. Moreover, Emperor Wei's wit and subtlety were no less than that of Emperor Jia. Unfortunately, Emperor Wu of the Jin Dynasty did not accept it after understanding it, resulting in the final failure.
