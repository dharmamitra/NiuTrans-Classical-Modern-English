古文：弟子规，圣人训。
现代文：《弟子规》这本是依据圣贤孔子的教诲编成的。
English: "Disciple Regulation" This book is based on the teachings of the sage Confucius.

古文：首孝悌，次谨信。
现代文：日常生活中，首先要孝顺父母，友爱兄弟姐妹。
English: In daily life, first of all, we should be filial to our parents and brothers and sisters.

古文：泛爱众，而亲仁。
现代文：其次，言语行为要小心谨慎，讲求信用。
English: Secondly, we should be careful in our speech and behavior, and seek credibility.

古文：有余力，则学文。
现代文：如有余下的时间精力，则应学习六艺及其他有益的学问。
English: If you have spare time and energy, you should study the Six Arts and other beneficial knowledge.
