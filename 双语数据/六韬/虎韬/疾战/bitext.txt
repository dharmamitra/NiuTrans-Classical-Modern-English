古文：武王问太公曰： 敌人围我，断我前后，绝我粮道，为之奈何？ 
现代文：武王询问太公道： 如果敌人从四面包围了我军，切断我军与外界的联系，断绝了我军运送粮草的道路，应该怎么办呢？ 
English: King Wu asked Tai Gongdao: What should we do if the enemy surrounded our army on all sides, cut off our army's contact with the outside world, and cut off our army's way of transporting grain and grass?

古文：太公曰： 此天下之困兵也。
现代文：太公答道： 这是天下处境最困难的军队。
English: The father-in-law replied, "This is the most difficult army in the world.

古文：暴用之则胜，徐用之则败。
现代文：在这种情况下，只有迅速突围摆脱困境，行动迟缓就会招致失败。
English: In this case, only a quick breakthrough out of the predicament, slow action will lead to failure.

古文：如此者，为四武冲陈，以武车骁骑，惊乱其军，而疾击之，可以横行。 
现代文：突围的方法是，把部队布成四面都有警戒的 四武冲阵 战斗队形，凭借战车和骁勇善战的骑兵，使敌人受到惊吓，使其陷入混乱，然后我军迅速突击，这样就可以横行无阻地突围出去了。 
English: The way to break out of the encirclement is to arrange the troops into a four-squadron battle formation with guards on all sides. With the help of chariots and brave cavalry, the enemy will be frightened and confused, and then our army will quickly attack, so that it can break out of the encirclement unimpeded.

古文：武王曰： 若已出围地，欲因以为胜，为之奈何？ 
现代文：武王问太公： 如果我军已成功地突出了敌军的包围，还想乘势击败敌军，又该怎么办呢？ 
English: King Wu asked the Grand Duke: If our army has successfully highlighted the encirclement of the enemy army and still wants to take advantage of the situation to defeat the enemy army, what should we do?

古文：太公曰： 左军疾左，右军疾右，无与敌人争道。中军迭前迭后，敌人员众，其将可走。 
现代文：太公答道： 应该指挥我左边的军队迅速向敌左翼发起攻击，指挥右边的军队迅速向敌右翼发起攻击，不要和敌人争夺道路以免分散兵力，同时中部的军队或前或后地轮番向敌人发动攻势，或击其前，或抄其后。
English: Taigong replied, "I should command the army on my left to quickly attack the enemy's left flank, and command the army on my right to quickly attack the enemy's right flank. Do not fight with the enemy for the road so as not to disperse the troops. At the same time, the army in the middle should take turns to launch an offensive against the enemy, either in front or behind, or hit the enemy in front or behind.
