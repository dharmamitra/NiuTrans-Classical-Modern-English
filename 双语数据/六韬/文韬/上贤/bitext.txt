古文：文王问太公曰： 王人者，何上，何下，何取，何去，何禁，何止？ 
现代文：文王问太公道： 作为君主，应当尊崇什么人，抑制什么人，任用什么人，去掉什么人？
English: King Wen asked Tai Rong Dao: As a monarch, who should be respected, who should be restrained, who should be appointed, and who should be removed?

古文：太公曰： 王人者，上贤，下不肖，取诚信，去诈伪，禁暴乱，止奢侈。
现代文：应该严禁什么事，制止什么事？ 太公回答说： 作为君主，应该尊崇德才兼备之人，抑制无德无才的人，任用忠诚信实之人，除去欺诈虚伪的人。严禁暴乱行为，制止奢侈风气。
English: What should be strictly prohibited and what should be stopped? The grand duke replied: As a monarch, one should respect those who are both virtuous and talented, restrain those who are neither virtuous nor talented, appoint those who are loyal and faithful, and remove those who are deceitful and hypocritical. Violent behavior is strictly prohibited, and the luxury culture should be stopped.

古文：故王人者，有六贼七害。 
现代文：所以君主应当警惕六贼、七害。 
English: Therefore, the monarch should be wary of six thieves and seven evils.

古文：文王曰： 愿闻其道。 
现代文：文王说： 说得对啊！ 
English: King Wen said, "That's right!
