古文：文王寝疾，召太公望，太子发在侧，曰： 呜呼！
现代文：文王卧病在床，召见太公，当时太子姬发业在一旁。文王说： 唉！
English: King Moon, who was ill in bed, summoned the Grand Duke. At the time, the Crown Prince and Empress Dowager was not there. King Moon said, "Alas!

古文：天将弃予，周之社稷将以属汝，今予欲师至道之言，以明传之子孙。 
现代文：恐怕上天要遗弃我了，周国的社稷大事就要托付给您了。现在我想听您讲讲至理明言，把安邦济世的方法，以便明确地传授给我的子孙。 
English: I am afraid that the heavens are about to abandon me, and the social and economic affairs of the surrounding country are about to be entrusted to you. Now I want to hear you speak the truth, and clearly teach my children and grandchildren the method of saving the world.

古文：太公曰： 王何所问？ 
现代文：太公问： 王您想要知道些什么呢？ 
English: The father-in-law asked, "What do you want to know, Wang?"

古文：文王曰： 先圣之道，其所止，其所起，可得闻乎？ 
现代文：文王说： 古代圣贤的治国之道，应该废弃什么，应该兴盛什么，您能够把其中的道理讲给我听听吗？ 
English: King Wen said, "The ancient sages' way of governing the country, what should be abandoned, what should prosper, can you tell me the reason?

古文：太公曰： 见善而怠，时至而疑，知非而处，此三者，道之所止也。
现代文：太公回答道： 看见善事却懈怠不去办，时机来临却迟疑不决，明知道有错还泰然处之，这三种情况就是先圣治国之道所应废止的。
English: The great-grandfather replied: Seeing a good deed but slacking off and not doing it, the time coming but hesitating, knowing that there is a mistake but dealing with it calmly, these three situations are the first to be abolished by the holy way of governing the country.

古文：柔而静，恭而敬，强而弱，忍而刚，此四者，道之所起也。
现代文：柔和而清静，谦恭而敬谨，强大而自居弱小，隐忍而能够刚正处事，这四种情况是先圣治国之道所应推行的。
English: To be meek and quiet, humble and reverent, strong and humble, patient and able to do justly, these four things are the first to be carried out in the way of a holy state.

古文：故义胜欲则昌，欲胜义则亡，敬胜怠则吉，怠胜敬则灭。 
现代文：所以，正义胜过私欲，国家就能昌盛；私欲胜过正义，国家就会走向灭亡；敬谨胜过懈怠，国家就能祥和；懈怠胜过敬谨，国家就会覆灭。 
English: Therefore, if justice prevails over selfishness, the country will prosper; if selfishness prevails over justice, the country will go to ruin; if respect prevails over slackness, the country will be peaceful; if slackness prevails over respect, the country will perish.
